Short Answer Task

Task:

Write 3 major differences between Android and iOS operating systems.

Answer Guidelines:

Use keywords like Open Source, Security, Customization, App Store/Play Store

Make your answer concise — around 30–40 words (as asked in Delhi Police descriptive-style questions)

Sample Answer Structure:

  • Open Source vs Closed Source - Android is open source, iOS is proprietary
  • App Distribution - Google Play Store vs Apple App Store
  • Customization - Android offers more flexibility, iOS has stricter controls
  • Device Compatibility - Android runs on multiple brands, iOS only on Apple devices
