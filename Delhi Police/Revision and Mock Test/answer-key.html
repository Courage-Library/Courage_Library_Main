Quick Exam Tricks & Recap

Key Focus Areas:

  • Memorize common acronyms (CPU, RAM, ROM, BIOS, LAN, WAN)
  • Understand difference between input/output devices
  • Practice Excel formulas & functions (SUM, AVERAGE, IF, COUNTIF)
  • Remember Word shortcuts: Ctrl+C, Ctrl+V, Ctrl+B

Exam Strategy:

  • Cybersecurity basics: Firewalls, antivirus, secure browsing
  • OS types & generations: Batch, multitasking, real-time
  • Charts & Graphs in Excel: Bar, Pie, Line
  • Focus on short, exam-level definitions

Pro Tip: Delhi Police exams often test the same concepts repeatedly. Master these key areas for high scores.
