Regional Kingdoms – Marathas, Vijayanagar, Rajputs, Sikh Kingdoms

Delhi Police Exam History Section

Introduction

After the decline of the Mughal Empire (esp after Aurangzeb), many strong regional powers emerged in India.

These kingdoms challenged centralized rule and prepared the background for British expansion later.

Maratha Empire

Chief architect: Shivaji

Capital: Raigad

Famous policies: Ashta Pradhan Council

Military system → Guerrilla warfare leadership

After Shivaji → Peshwas became powerful

3 Anglo-Maratha Wars (→ Cause for decline)

Important Peshwas: Balaji Vishwanath, Bajirao I, Balaji Bajirao (Nana Saheb)

Vijayanagara Empire

Founded in 1336 by Harihara & Bukka (Sangama dynasty)

Capital: Vijayanagar (Hampi) – modern Karnataka

Flourished during Krishna Deva Raya (Tuluva Dynasty)

Defeated by combined Deccan Sultanates at Battle of Talikota (1565)

Architecture style: Dravidian temples (e.g., Vittala Temple)

Rajput Kingdoms

Rajputs played major role in North India

Rana Pratap famous for Haldighati Battle (1576) against Akbar

Rajputs later signed alliances & matrimonial relations with Mughals (esp Akbar)

Important Rajput States: Mewar (Rana Sanga, Rana Pratap), Marwar (Jodhpur), Amber/Jaipur (Kachwaha)

Sikh Kingdom (Punjab Region)

Formation roots in Sikh Gurus (10 Gurus)

Khalsa created by Guru Gobind Singh (1699)

Founder of Sikh Empire: Maharaja Ranjit Singh (1799)

Capital: Lahore

Built strong modern army (European style)

Treaty of Amritsar (1809) with British

Sikh empire ended after Anglo-Sikh Wars

QUICK RECAP (5 Points)

Shivaji → founder of Maratha Empire, guerrilla warfare specialist

Vijayanagar → powerful southern empire, defeated at Talikota (1565)

Krishna Deva Raya → greatest Vijayanagar ruler

Rajputs resisted + allied with Mughals at different times

Sikh Empire founded by Maharaja Ranjit Singh at Lahore

Sample MCQs

1

The founder of Vijayanagar Empire was:

Options: (a) Harihara & Bukka (b) Shivaji (c) Ranjit Singh (d) Rana Sanga

View Answer

A. Harihara & Bukka

2

Who founded the Sikh Empire?

Options: (a) Guru Arjan (b) Guru Gobind Singh (c) Ranjit Singh (d) Rana Pratap

View Answer

C. Ranjit Singh

3

Battle of Talikota resulted in the fall of which empire?

Options: (a) Mughal (b) Vijayanagar (c) Maratha (d) Rajput

View Answer

B. Vijayanagar
