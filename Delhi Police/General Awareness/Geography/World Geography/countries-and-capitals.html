Major Countries and Capitals of the World

World Geography – Exam Preparation

Introduction: Understanding the Global Map

Every country in the world has a capital city — the administrative, political, and cultural center of the nation.

For exams like Delhi Police, SSC, and other government tests, knowing important countries and their capitals is a must for the GK section.

Memory Trick: "Capitals are the heartbeat of countries. Learn them continent by continent!"
