National Sports Events of India

Delhi Police Exams - GK Section

Introduction

India’s sports ecosystem is filled with historic tournaments, modern leagues, and national awards that inspire athletes and unite the nation. From traditional games like Kabaddi to professional leagues like ISL and PKL, India celebrates sports at every level.

Memory Trick: N–K–T–L–A → National Games, Khelo India, Trophies, Leagues, Awards.

National Games of India

Organized By Indian Olympic Association (IOA)
First Held 1924 (Lahore) – Indian Olympic Games
Renamed As National Games (1940s)
Frequency Every 2–4 years
Modeled On Olympic Games
Latest Edition (37th) 2023 – Goa
Next Host (38th) Uttarakhand (2025)

Motto: Unity Through Sports

Sports Included: Athletics, Hockey, Boxing, Wrestling, Shooting, Swimming, etc.

Medal Toppers (2023):
1st – Services | 2nd – Maharashtra | 3rd – Haryana

National Games: India’s Own Olympics.

Khelo India Youth Games

Launched ByMinistry of Youth Affairs & Sports
Year2018
ObjectiveIdentify & nurture young sporting talent
Organized BySports Authority of India (SAI)
MottoKheloge Kudoge Banoge Lajawaab
CategoriesUnder-17 and Under-21
Recent Host2024 – Tamil Nadu

Major Aim: Scholarships & training via the Khelo India Talent Development Programme

Trick: Khelo India = Catch them young, train them strong.

India’s Major Sports Awards

Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award (Now: Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna)

Introduced1991–92
Renamed2021 (in honour of Dhyan Chand)
Given ForHighest sporting honour for outstanding international performance
Award IncludesMedallion, Certificate, ₹25 lakh cash
Recent WinnersNeeraj Chopra, Mirabai Chanu

Khel Ratna = Crown of Indian Sports.

Arjuna Award

Introduced1961
Given ForOutstanding performance over 4 years
Award IncludesBronze statuette, Certificate, ₹15 lakh
Recent WinnersHarmanpreet Kaur, Lakshya Sen, Rani Rampal

Arjuna = Aim True, Honour True.

Dronacharya Award

Introduced1985
Given ToCoaches producing medalists
Award IncludesStatuette, Certificate, ₹15 lakh
Recent WinnersRadhakrishnan Nair, Jaspal Rana

Dronacharya trains Arjuna to win Khel Ratna.

Dhyan Chand Award

Introduced2002
Given ForLifetime contribution to sports
Recent WinnersTrupti Murgunde, Harbinder Singh

Dhyan Chand = Devotion for Life in Sports.

National Sports Day

Date29th August
SignificanceBirth anniversary of Major Dhyan Chand
PurposePromotion & honouring of sports
Awards CeremonyPresented by President of India

Memory Tip: 29 August – Stick of Gold Day.

PYQs (Delhi Police, SSC & State Exams)

The National Games of India were first held in —

A) 1947   B) 1924   C) 1951   D) 1934

A) Play for India   B) Kheloge Kudoge Banoge Lajawaab   C) Let’s Play India   D) Youth for Sports
