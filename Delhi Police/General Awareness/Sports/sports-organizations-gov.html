Sports Organizations & Governing Bodies

Delhi Police Exams - GK Section

Introduction

Every sport is governed by a dedicated body or federation that regulates rules, organizes tournaments, and ensures fair play. These organizations conduct championships, manage athletes, and promote sports development worldwide and in India.

Memory Trick: I–B–F–I–F–A–I → IOC, BCCI, FIFA, ICC, FIH, AIFF, IOA.

International Olympic Committee (IOC)

Full FormInternational Olympic Committee
Founded1894 (Paris)
FounderPierre de Coubertin
HeadquartersLausanne, Switzerland
President (2025)Thomas Bach
MottoCitius, Altius, Fortius – Communiter

Functions:

  • Organizes Summer & Winter Olympic Games
  • Promotes international peace and sportsmanship
  • Recognizes National Olympic Committees (ex: IOA, India)
  • Enforces anti-doping & athlete welfare measures

IOC = Olympics Organizer & Controller.

Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI)

Founded1928
HeadquartersMumbai
TypeNational Governing Body (Private Autonomous)
President (2025)Roger Binny
SecretaryJay Shah
Affiliated ToICC

Functions:

  • Organizes domestic tournaments: Ranji, Duleep, IPL
  • Selects Indian cricket teams (Men, Women, U-19)
  • Controls cricket administration, coaching, funding
  • Represents India at ICC meets & global events

BCCI = Boss of Cricket in India.

Fédération Internationale de Football Association (FIFA)

Full FormFédération Internationale de Football Association
Founded1904 (Paris)
HeadquartersZurich, Switzerland
President (2025)Gianni Infantino
Members211 National Associations

Functions:

  • Organizes FIFA World Cups (Men & Women, 4 years)
  • Frames rules of football (Laws of the Game)
  • Promotes football development & fair play worldwide
  • Supervises continental confederations (Ex: AFC Asia)

FIFA = Football’s International Family Association.

International Cricket Council (ICC)

Founded1909 (Imperial Cricket Conference)
Renamed1989 (International Cricket Council)
HeadquartersDubai, UAE
Members108 (12 Full + 96 Associate)
Chairman (2025)Greg Barclay
CEOGeoff Allardice

Functions:

  • Governs ODIs, T20s, Test matches internationally
  • Organizes World Cup, T20 WC, WTC
  • Sets standards, rankings, umpire rules
  • Works for anti-corruption & anti-doping

ICC = International Cricket Controller.

International Hockey Federation (FIH)

Full FormFédération Internationale de Hockey
Founded1924 (Paris)
HeadquartersLausanne, Switzerland
President (2025)Tayyab Ikram
Members140+ Countries

Functions:

  • Organizes Hockey World Cups (Men, Women)
  • Sets rules, umpiring standards in hockey
  • Promotes hockey through events & training
  • FIH Academy for coaching & youth programs

FIH = Field Hockey’s International Home.

All India Football Federation (AIFF)

Founded1937
HeadquartersNew Delhi
Affiliated ToFIFA & AFC
President (2025)Kalyan Chaubey
SecretaryShaji Prabhakaran

Functions:

  • Controls football in India
  • Organizes Santosh Trophy, I-League, ISL (with FSDL)
  • Manages Indian national football teams
  • Grassroots & youth football development

AIFF = All India’s Football Family.

Indian Olympic Association (IOA)

Founded1927
HeadquartersNew Delhi
President (2025)P.T. Usha
Affiliated ToInternational Olympic Committee (IOC)

Functions:

  • Represents India at Olympics, Asian, Commonwealth Games
  • Selects and sends Indian athletes to world events
  • Organizes National Games of India
  • Works with SAI & Sports Ministry to promote Olympics

IOA = India’s Olympic Ambassador.

Final Concept Recap

  • BCCI (1928) → Governs Indian cricket
  • FIFA (1904) → Controls world football
  • ICC (1909) → Controls world cricket
  • FIH (1924) → Controls world hockey
  • AIFF (1937) → Controls Indian football
  • IOA (1927) → Represents India at Olympics
