Sports Terminologies & Vocabulary

Delhi Police Exams - GK Section

Introduction

Each sport has its own unique vocabulary or technical terms that describe gameplay, rules, and scoring. Knowing these terms is vital for sports-related GK, especially for exam MCQs.

Memory Trick: C–F–T–B–A–H → Cricket, Football, Tennis, Badminton, Athletics, Hockey.

Cricket – The Gentleman’s Game

Term Meaning
LBW (Leg Before Wicket)Batsman out if leg prevents a ball that would hit the stumps
Maiden OverOver with zero runs conceded
YorkerBall bowled at the batsman's feet
PowerplayRestricted fielding period in ODIs/T20s
DuckBatsman scores zero runs
Hat-trickBowler takes 3 wickets in 3 balls

Trick: LBW–Maiden–Yorker–Power = Cricket Power Combo.

Football – The World’s Game

Term Meaning
OffsideMore advanced than ball/defender at play moment
Penalty KickFree kick from penalty spot after a foul in area
GoalpostGoal structure for scoring
Corner KickAwarded when defender last touches ball out over goal line
DribbleSkillful ball control and movement with feet
Yellow Card / Red CardYellow = Warning; Red = Expulsion

Offside, Penalty, Corner – Football’s OPC Rule.

Tennis – The Game of Sets and Serves

Term Meaning
Deuce40–40 score; win by 2 consecutive points
AceUnreturnable serve
Break PointReceiver’s chance to win on opponent’s serve
SetUnit of scoring: first to 6 games, 2-game lead
LoveZero score
VolleyHitting ball before it bounces

Deuce–Ace–Break–Set–Love → Tennis Terms DABSL.

Badminton – The Fastest Racket Game

Term Meaning
Drop ShotShot just clearing net, lands close to it
SmashPowerful overhead shot
ShuttleProjectile used in badminton
RallyContinuous play until point is scored
Net ShotDelicate shot played close to net

Drop–Smash–Shuttle = Badminton’s 3D Formula.

Athletics – The Track and Field Domain

Term Meaning
MarathonDistance race (42.195 km)
RelayTeam race passing baton
HurdleRace with jump barriers
DecathlonCombined event – 10 events
SprintShort-distance race (100/200/400 m)

MRH – Marathon, Relay, Hurdle = Athletics Basics.

Hockey – India’s Pride Game

Term Meaning
DribbleSkillful ball movement with stick
PushPassing ball on ground with stick push
Penalty CornerAwarded for defensive foul in circle
ScoopLifting ball over opponent’s stick
FlickQuick wrist shot

Dribble–Push–Corner – Hockey’s DPC Rule.
