Environmental & Awareness Days

Static GK for Exams – Nature, Biodiversity, and Climate

Introduction to Environmental Days

Environmental days are celebrated globally to inspire awareness about nature, wildlife, forests, oceans, and how we can help sustain the planet. Frequently asked in exams under General Awareness, their significance covers current affairs, global hosts, and yearly themes.

Memory Trick: "Wild Forest Earth Oceans Need Ozone" (Wildlife → Forest → Earth → Oceans → Nature → Ozone)
Exam Focus: Know the date, host country, organizing body, and theme for each event.

World Wildlife Day

Category Details
Date 3rd March
Organized By United Nations (UN)
Objective Raise awareness of the world's wild animals/plants
Adopted By UN General Assembly (2013)
Significance Marks signing of CITES (1973)
Theme (2024): "Connecting People and Planet: Exploring Digital Innovation in Wildlife Conservation."
Trick: "3rd March – 3 for the 3: Wildlife, Forests, Future."

World Forest Day (International Day of Forests)

Category Details
Date 21st March
Organized By UN FAO
Objective Awareness on role of forests and trees
Started In 2012 (UNGA)
Theme (2024): "Forests and Innovation: New Solutions for a Better World."
Trick: "21st March – Trees Teach Us Balance Before Earth Day."

Earth Day

Category Details
Date 22nd April
Started In 1970
Organized By Earth Day Network
Objective Support environmental protection
Theme (2024): "Planet vs Plastics."
Trick: "22 April – Save the Only Earth We Have."
Quick Fact: Earth Day led to the creation of EPA (USA) in 1970.

World Environment Day

Category Details
Date 5th June
Established By UNEP (1972)
First Celebrated In 1974
Host (2024) Saudi Arabia
Objective Promote protection & sustainability
Theme (2024): "Land Restoration, Desertification & Drought Resilience."
Trick: "5 June – 5 Elements of Life: Earth, Air, Water, Fire, Space."

World Oceans Day

Category Details
Date 8th June
Recognized By UN (since 2008)
Objective Awareness of oceans & marine life
Theme (2024): "Awaken New Depths."
Trick: "8th June – Blue Planet's Birthday."
Fact: Oceans cover 70% of Earth and produce 50% of oxygen.

World Nature Conservation Day

Category Details
Date 28th July
Objective Conservation of nature & resources
Theme (2024): "Forests and Livelihoods: Sustaining People and Planet."
Trick: "July 28 – Nature Never Waits, Conserve Today!"

World Ozone Day

Category Details
Date 16th September
Established By UNGA (1994)
Significance Marks signing of Montreal Protocol (1987)
Objective Raise awareness to protect the ozone layer
Theme (2024): "Montreal Protocol: Keeping Us, Food & Vaccines Cool."
Trick: "Ozone Protects – We Protect Ozone. (16 Sept)"

  • World Forest Day (21 March) – Importance of forests
  • Earth Day (22 April) – Climate change awareness
