  • Sender's address
  • Date
  • Receiver's address
  • Subject line
  • Salutation

3. Quick Recap - Key Shortcuts

File Operations: Ctrl+N (New), Ctrl+S (Save), Ctrl+Shift+S (Save As), Ctrl+P (Print)

Editing: Ctrl+C (Copy), Ctrl+X (Cut), Ctrl+V (Paste), Ctrl+Z (Undo)

Formatting: Ctrl+B (Bold), Ctrl+I (Italic), Ctrl+U (Underline)

Navigation: Ctrl+A (Select All), Ctrl+F (Find), Ctrl+H (Replace)

Function Keys: F7 (Spell Check), F12 (Save As)

Important: Alt+N→P (Page Number), Table Tools→Merge Cells

Tip: These shortcuts and features are highly frequent in Delhi Police and SSC computer exams.
