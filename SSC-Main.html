Understanding SSC Exam Structure

Tier I

Objective-type test covering General Intelligence, Quantitative Aptitude, English, and General Awareness.

Tier II

Descriptive or advanced paper focusing on reasoning, quantitative skills, and English comprehension.

Tier III

Skill or Computer Proficiency Test depending on the specific SSC exam.
