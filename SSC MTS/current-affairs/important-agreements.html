Climate & Environment Agreements (SSC MOST IMPORTANT)

1. Paris Climate Agreement (2015)

Goal

Limit global warming to 1.5°C

Key Mechanism

NDCs (Nationally Determined Contributions)

India's Commitment

• Reduce carbon intensity
• Increase renewable energy capacity

2. Kyoto Protocol (1997)

Purpose

Reduce greenhouse gas emissions

Binding Targets

For developed countries only

India's Status

No binding targets (developing nation)

3. Montreal Protocol (1987)

Purpose

Protect Ozone Layer

Key Ban

CFCs (Chlorofluorocarbons)

Recovery Expected

Ozone layer to heal by 2050

4. Convention on Biological Diversity (CBD)

Purpose

Protect global biodiversity

India's Status

Signatory and active participant

Memory Trick: PMK = Paris, Montreal, Kyoto (Major Climate Agreements)

Trade & Economic Agreements

WTO Agreements

Full Form

World Trade Organization

Key Agreements

• GATT (Goods trade)
• GATS (Services trade)
• TRIPS (Intellectual property)

India's Recent FTAs

India-UAE CEPA (2022)

Comprehensive Economic Partnership Agreement

India-Australia ECTA

Economic Cooperation & Trade Agreement

India-Mauritius CECPA

Comprehensive Economic Cooperation & Partnership Agreement

QUAD Economic Cooperation

• Members: India, USA, Japan, Australia
• Focus: Indo-Pacific economic stability
• Areas: Defence, cyber security, semiconductors

RCEP (Important for SSC)

Full Form

Regional Comprehensive Economic Partnership

World's Largest Trade Pact

Includes ASEAN+5 countries

India's Position

Did NOT join (Important fact for SSC)

Memory Trick: UAM = UAE, Australia, Mauritius (India's recent FTA partners)

Peace & Security Agreements

India-USA Defence Agreements

COMCASA

Communications Compatibility & Security Agreement

LEMOA

Logistics Exchange Memorandum of Agreement

BECA

Basic Exchange & Cooperation Agreement

Other Key Defence Agreements

Malabar Naval Exercise

India + USA + Japan + Australia (Quad naval cooperation)

India-France Rafale Agreement

Purchase of Rafale fighter jets for Indian Air Force

India-Russia S-400 Deal

Advanced missile defence system acquisition

Memory Trick: CLB = COMCASA, LEMOA, BECA (India-USA defence agreements)

India's Major Bilateral Agreements

India–UAE CEPA

India–Japan Agreements

Bullet train project, defence cooperation

India–France Agreements

Space, Rafale jets, strategic partnership

India–Bangladesh Agreements

Teesta water talks, trade, border cooperation

Human Rights, Peace & Global Health Agreements

Universal Declaration of Human Rights (UDHR)

• Adopted: 1948 by UN
• Purpose: Guarantee human rights globally
• Status: Foundation of international human rights law

WHO Pandemic Agreements

• Global health emergency response
• COVID-19 pandemic frameworks
• International disease control cooperation

Nuclear Non-Proliferation Treaty (NPT)

• Purpose: Prevent nuclear weapons spread
• Signed: 1968, effective 1970
• India's Status: NOT a member

SSC Practice Questions

1. Paris Agreement aims to limit global warming to?

1.5°C

2. Which treaty bans CFCs to protect ozone layer?

Montreal Protocol

3. Kyoto Protocol deals with?

Greenhouse gas reduction for developed nations

4. Which major trade bloc did India NOT join?

RCEP (Regional Comprehensive Economic Partnership)

5. COMCASA is a defence agreement between India and?

USA

6. S-400 missile defence system was purchased from?

Russia

7. Rafale fighter jets came from which country?

France

8. Which agreement protects the ozone layer?

Montreal Protocol

9. NPT stands for?

Nuclear Non-Proliferation Treaty

10. India-UAE trade agreement is called?

CEPA (Comprehensive Economic Partnership Agreement)

Conclusion

Mastering Important International Agreements is crucial for SSC current affairs preparation. These topics are consistently asked in competitive exams.

Key Takeaways

Paris Agreement: 1.5°C warming limit, NDCs
Montreal Protocol: CFC ban, ozone protection
Kyoto Protocol: GHG cuts for developed nations
India-UAE CEPA: Major trade agreement
India-USA Defence: COMCASA, LEMOA, BECA
S-400 Deal: India-Russia missile defence
Rafale Deal: India-France fighter jets
RCEP: World's largest trade pact, India didn't join
NPT: Nuclear non-proliferation, India not a member
WTO: Global trade rules (GATT, GATS, TRIPS)
