Memory Tricks for SSC

Nature Days Pattern

March-June: Water (22 Mar), Forest (21 Mar), Earth (22 Apr), Environment (5 Jun), Yoga (21 Jun)

Trick: 3-21-22-22-5-21 pattern

Health Days Sequence

Cancer (4 Feb), TB (24 Mar), Health (7 Apr), No Tobacco (31 May), AIDS (1 Dec)

Trick: 4-24-7-31-1 for important diseases

Indian National Days

Youth (12 Jan), Republic (26 Jan), Sports (29 Aug), Gandhi (2 Oct), Education (11 Nov)

Trick: 12-26-29-2-11 sequence

Environmental Days

All environment-related days concentrated in first half of year

Remember: June = Green (Environment Day)
