Key Takeaways

Khel Ratna: Highest award, named after Dhyan Chand
Arjuna Award: For 4 years consistent performance
Dronacharya Award: For coaches producing champions
Dhyan Chand Award: Lifetime achievement for retired players
Award Date: Given on 29 August (National Sports Day)
Neeraj Chopra: Khel Ratna winner for javelin
Mohammad Shami: Recent Arjuna Award winner
Memory Trick: K-A-D-D (Highest to Lifetime)
International: Laureus, Ballon d'Or, FIFA Awards
ICC Award: Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy
