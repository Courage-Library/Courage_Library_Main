Major International Summits

Current Affairs - Lesson 2: Complete Notes for SSC MTS, GD, CHSL

What Are International Summits?

International Summits = Global meetings where leaders discuss economy, climate, peace, trade, technology & development

G20 Summit (VERY IMPORTANT)

G20

Group of 20 Major Economies

G20 Summit 2023 – New Delhi, India

Theme

"Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam – One Earth, One Family, One Future"

Key Outcomes

• African Union became permanent member
• Global Biofuel Alliance launched
• Showcased India's global leadership

Future G20 Summits

2024 Host

Brazil

2025 Host

South Africa

India's Presidency

2023 was India's presidency year

Memory Trick: IBS → India → Brazil → South Africa (G20 host sequence)

COP Climate Summit (SUPER IMPORTANT)

COP = Conference of Parties (UN Climate Summit)

COP28 (2023)

Host

Dubai, UAE

Key Decisions

• First-ever Global Stocktake
• Transition away from fossil fuels
• Climate finance increased

COP29 (2024)

Host

Azerbaijan

Focus Areas

Climate finance, adaptation, mitigation

India's Role

Active participant, advocate for developing nations

Memory Trick: UAE → Azerbaijan (alphabetical order for recent COP hosts)

QUAD Summit

QUAD Members

USA

Key Information

Focus Areas

• Indo-Pacific security
• Technology cooperation
• Maritime security

Recent Meeting

Tokyo (2022), virtual meetings in 2023

Other Important Summits

ASEAN Summit

• 10 Southeast Asian nations
• 2024 Host: Laos
• 2023 Host: Indonesia
• India: Strategic Partner

UNGA (UN General Assembly)

• Held every year in New York
• Discusses global peace, development
• Indian PM addresses annually
• All UN members participate

World Economic Forum (WEF)

• Annual meeting in Davos, Switzerland
• World leaders discuss economy
• Focus: Technology, global challenges
• Indian participation at highest level

SSC Practice Questions

India

2. Where was COP28 held?

Dubai, UAE

3. BRICS 2024 summit host country?

Russia

4. ASEAN Summit 2024 host country?

Laos

5. WEF annual meeting venue?

Davos, Switzerland

6. SCO members include which major countries?

India, China, Russia, Pakistan

7. African Union became a permanent member of which group?

G20

8. What does QUAD include?

India, USA, Japan, Australia

New York, USA

Conference of Parties

Conclusion

Mastering Major International Summits is crucial for SSC current affairs preparation. These topics are consistently asked in competitive exams.

Key Takeaways

G20 2023: India hosted, theme "Vasudhaiva Kutumbakam"
BRICS: Expanded with Saudi Arabia, UAE, Ethiopia, Egypt
SCO: 2024 host Kazakhstan, includes India, China, Russia
COP28: UAE hosted, transition away from fossil fuels
COP29: Azerbaijan to host in 2024
QUAD: India, USA, Japan, Australia - Indo-Pacific focus
ASEAN: 10 Southeast Asian nations, 2024 host Laos
UNGA: Annual New York meeting, all UN members
WEF: Annual Davos meeting, global economic forum
African Union: New permanent G20 member from 2023
