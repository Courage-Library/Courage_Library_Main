Southern Kingdoms – Cholas, Cheras & Pandyas

Complete Notes for SSC MTS Exams - Ancient South India

Introduction: The Three Tamil Kingdoms (Muvendar)

The Chola, Chera and Pandya kingdoms are called Muvendar (Tamil word) meaning "Three crowned kings of ancient Tamil Nadu".

1. The Cholas – The Most Powerful Southern Kingdom

Region:

  • Mainly Tamil Nadu, especially along Kaveri river
  • Famous city: Thanjavur (Tanjore)

Early Cholas

Greatest early ruler: Karikala Chola

  • Built Kallanai Dam (Grand Anicut) on Kaveri
  • One of the oldest dams in the world still in use

Memory Trick:

Karikala → Kaveri → Kallanai (K–K–K → easy chain!)

Imperial Cholas (Very Important)

Rajaraja Chola I (985–1014 CE)

  • Expanded Chola empire over Sri Lanka
  • Built the Brihadeshwara Temple at Thanjavur
  • UNESCO World Heritage Site

Rajendra Chola I

  • Son of Rajaraja
  • Conquered Southeast Asia (Indonesia, Malaysia)
  • Performed Gangaikonda Chola expedition
  • Founded Gangaikonda Cholapuram

Features of Chola Administration

Most organized administration in South India.

Village System (Very Important)

Villages were self-governed through Sabhas and Ur committees.

Local Self-Government

Most questions in SSC come from this point.

Ports:

Nagapattinam
Poompuhar
Kaveripattinam

Chola Art & Architecture

Dravidian style temples

Tall gopurams

Big temple complexes

Nataraja bronze statues

2. The Cheras – The Kings of Kerala

Region

  • Modern Kerala + parts of Tamil Nadu
  • Ruled from the west coast

Important Rulers

Uthiyan Cheralathan

Earliest known ruler

Senguttuvan (Red Chera) - MOST IMPORTANT for SSC

Achievements:
  • Built Kannagi (Pattini) temple
  • Associated with Silappadikaram (Tamil epic)
Memory Trick:

"Chera = Kerala"

(Same sound pattern)

Chera Symbols

Bow and Arrow

This is a direct SSC question

Ports of Cheras

Muziris (Most Important)

  • Known for spice trade
  • Traded with Romans
  • Pepper was called Black Gold

Tondi

Vanji (capital)

Quick Revision: Cheras

Feature Details

"Chera uses Bow n Arrow → Shoot spices to Rome!"

Most Expected MCQs (SSC MTS)

1. Kallanai Dam was built by?

Karikala Chola

2. Symbol of Pandyas?

Fish

3. Senguttuvan was a king of which dynasty?

Cheras

4. The famous port Muziris belongs to?

Chera dynasty

5. Brihadeshwara Temple was built by?

Rajaraja Chola I

6. The Chola system of village administration was known for?

Local self-government (Sabhas)
