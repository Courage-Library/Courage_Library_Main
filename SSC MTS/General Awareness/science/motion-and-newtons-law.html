Motion & Newton's Laws of Motion

Physics - Complete Notes for SSC MTS, GD, CHSL

What is Motion?

An object is in motion if it changes its position with respect to time

Examples of Motion

Moving car on road
Person walking
Earth revolving around sun
Fan blades rotating

Important Concept

REST

When object does NOT change its position

Motion is always relative to a reference point

Types of Motion

1. Rectilinear Motion

Straight-line motion

Example: Car on straight road

2. Circular Motion

Motion in a circle

Example: Fan blades, Planets

3. Oscillatory Motion

Back-and-forth motion

Example: Pendulum, Swing

4. Periodic Motion

Repeats after equal intervals

Example: Heartbeat, Clock pendulum

Newton's First Law of Motion (Law of Inertia)

"A body at rest will remain at rest, and a body in motion will remain in motion unless an external force acts on it."
