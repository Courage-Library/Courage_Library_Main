4. Common Rules to Remember for Correct Spelling

1. 'i' before 'e' except after 'c'

Example: Believe, Receive

2. Double the last consonant when adding a vowel suffix

3. Drop the final 'e' before adding '-ing' or '-ed'

4. Change 'y' to 'i' before adding '-es' or '-ed'

5. Words ending in '-ful' never have a double 'l'

Example: Beautiful, Careful
