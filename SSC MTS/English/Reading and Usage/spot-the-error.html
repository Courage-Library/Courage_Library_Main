Spot the Error (English Language)

SSC MTS & Delhi Police Exams - English Language

1. What is 'Spot the Error'?

In this type of question, a sentence is divided into three or four parts. You must identify which part has an error (grammatical or structural). If the sentence is correct, choose "No Error."

2. Tips to Solve 'Spot the Error' Questions

1. Read the Full Sentence Carefully

Understand the complete context before identifying errors.

2. Check Subject-Verb Agreement

Ensure subject and verb match in number and person.

3. Look for Tense Consistency

Check if tenses are used correctly throughout the sentence.

4. Watch Prepositions & Articles

Pay attention to correct preposition and article usage.

5. Check Idioms & Fixed Phrases

Identify correct idiomatic expressions like "fond of".

6. Verify Plural/Singular Forms

Ensure proper use of singular and plural nouns with determiners.
