Sentence Improvement (English Language)

SSC MTS & Delhi Police Exams - English Language

1. What is Sentence Improvement?

In this type of question, a sentence is given with a part underlined, and you must choose the best version (improved form) from the given options. If no improvement is needed, you select "No improvement."

2. Tips to Solve Sentence Improvement Questions

1. Read Carefully

Read the full sentence carefully â€“ understand the meaning first.

2. Identify Errors

Identify grammatical errors â€“ verb tense, agreement, preposition, etc.

3. Eliminate Options

4. Focus on Usage

Focus on usage â€“ some phrases are idiomatic (e.g., "fond of").

5. Choose Natural Sound

If two options seem right, choose the more natural one.

6. Check Tense Consistency

Ensure tense consistency throughout the sentence.
