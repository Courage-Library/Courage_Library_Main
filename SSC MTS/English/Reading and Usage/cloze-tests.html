Cloze Test (English Language)

SSC MTS & Delhi Police Exams - English Language

1. What is a Cloze Test?

A Cloze Test is a paragraph with blanks where you must choose the most suitable word for each blank from the given options.

Key Skills Tested:
â€¢ Grammar â€¢ Vocabulary â€¢ Comprehension â€¢ Context Understanding

Example Format:

"A good student is always ______ about learning new things. He ______ to acquire knowledge..."

2. Tips to Solve Cloze Test

1. Read the Whole Passage First

Get the theme and tone before filling blanks.

2. Understand Context

Identify whether it's formal, narrative, or informative.

3. Use Grammar Clues

Check tense, prepositions, articles, conjunctions.

4. Eliminate Wrong Options

Remove options that don't fit grammatically or logically.

5. Look for Collocations

Identify common word pairings like "make a decision".

6. Stay Consistent

Maintain consistent tense and voice throughout.
