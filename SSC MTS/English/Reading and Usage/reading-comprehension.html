Reading Comprehension (English Language)

SSC MTS & Delhi Police Exams - English Language

1. What is Reading Comprehension?

Reading Comprehension (RC) tests your ability to understand, interpret, and analyze a passage. You will be given a short passage followed by questions based on its content, vocabulary, and meaning.

Key Point:
RC passages can be on any topic - science, environment, social issues, technology, or general awareness.
