1. What is Cyber Security?

Cyber Security refers to the practice of protecting computers, servers, mobile devices, electronic systems, networks, and data from malicious attacks, damage, or unauthorized access. It's also known as information technology security.

6. Types of Malware

Malware Classification

Trojan Horse

Disguised as legitimate software

Creates backdoors, steals data

Example: Zeus, Emotet

Ransomware

Encrypts files for ransom

Demands payment to restore access

Example: WannaCry, Petya

Spyware

Secretly monitors activity

Collects personal information

Example: Keyloggers, Adware

Adware

Displays unwanted ads

Generates revenue for attackers

Example: Fireball, Gator

Malware (Malicious Software) includes viruses, worms, trojans, ransomware, spyware, and adware - all designed to damage or gain unauthorized access to computer systems.
