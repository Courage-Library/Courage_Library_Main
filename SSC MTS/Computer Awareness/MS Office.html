MS Office

SSC MTS Exam

1. What is Microsoft Office?

Microsoft Office is a suite of productivity applications developed by Microsoft. It includes various software programs, services, and servers that help users create documents, spreadsheets, presentations, and more for personal and professional use.

Productivity Suite

Document Creation

Data Analysis

Presentation Tools

Simple Definition: MS Office is a collection of software applications used for office work and personal productivity.

Courage Tip: Remember that Word is for documents (.docx), Excel is for spreadsheets and calculations (.xlsx), and PowerPoint is for presentations (.pptx). Keyboard shortcuts like Ctrl+S (Save), Ctrl+C (Copy), and Ctrl+V (Paste) work across all MS Office applications.
