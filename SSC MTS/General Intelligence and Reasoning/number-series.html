Number Series

SSC MTS Exam Section

Concept

In number series a sequence of numbers is given and we identify the pattern and find the next term / missing term.

Patterns are mostly:

Addition / Subtraction

Multiplication / Division

Square / Cube pattern

Prime numbers

Alternating pattern

Mix logic (+ then × etc)

Exam Tip: Always start with simple addition/subtraction patterns before moving to complex ones.

How to Detect Pattern Fast (Priority Order)

Step 1

Check small difference → + / -

Step 2

Check × / ÷ pattern

Step 3

Check square / cube related jumps

Step 4

Check prime numbers

Step 5

Check alternate series (very common in SSC MTS)

Solved Examples (Short Answer)

2, 4, 6, 8, ?

+2 pattern → next = 10

3, 9, 27, 81, ?

×3 pattern → next = 243

5, 11, 17, 23, ?

+6 pattern → next = 29

2, 5, 10, 17, 26, ?

+3, +5, +7, +9 → next +11 → 37

Always try + / - first

Then try × / ÷ patterns

Then check squares / cubes

Watch for alternating pattern

MTS usually keeps numbers small

Pattern must be simple in SSC
