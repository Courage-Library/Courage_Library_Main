Ranking & Ordering

Height / Weight / Position - SSC MTS Exam Section

Concept

Ranking questions check comparison between people/objects based on height, weight, marks, age, position etc. The logic is based on left-right ranking, total number, and inside positioning.

Exam Tip: Always remember the key formula: Total = Left Rank + Right Rank - 1. This solves most ranking problems quickly.

Common Types of Ranking

Type Example Pattern
Left / Right Ranking Position from left or right side
Total Candidates Finding Total = Left Rank + Right Rank – 1
Middle Position Finding Middle = (Total + 1) / 2
Comparison Ranking Who is taller / heavier / older etc
Ordering Logic Arrange in sequence based on conditions

Important Formulas

Case Formula
Total persons Left Rank + Right Rank – 1
Position from right Total – Left Rank + 1
Position from left Total – Right Rank + 1

Solved Examples

A is 7th from the left and 10th from the right. How many persons are there?

Total = 7 + 10 – 1 = 16

B is 9th from left in a row of 20. What is his position from right?

= 20 – 9 + 1 = 12th

In height order, P is taller than Q but shorter than R. Who is tallest?

→ R

Practice Questions

1

A is 12th from left and 9th from right. Total persons = ?

Options: a) 21 b) 20 c) 19 d) 22

Use formula: Total = Left Rank + Right Rank - 1

View Answer

b) 20

Total = 12 + 9 - 1 = 20

2

In a class, M is 8th from left. Total students are 30. Position of M from right?

Options: a) 22 b) 23 c) 21 d) 24

Use formula: Position from right = Total - Left Rank + 1

View Answer

b) 23

Position from right = 30 - 8 + 1 = 23

3

In weight order: R > S > T > U. Who is lightest?

Options: a) R b) S c) T d) U

Read the comparison symbols carefully

View Answer

d) U

R > S > T > U means U is lightest

4

P is older than Q but younger than N. Who is middle?

Options: a) N b) P c) Q d) Cannot determine

Arrange in order: N > P > Q

View Answer

b) P

Order: N (oldest) > P (middle) > Q (youngest)

5

In a row, if B is 5th from left and 14th from right, how many persons?

Options: a) 17 b) 18 c) 19 d) 20

Use formula: Total = Left Rank + Right Rank - 1

View Answer

b) 18

Total = 5 + 14 - 1 = 18

Quick Recap

Ranking = positioning logic + left/right calculation

Total = Left + Right – 1 always

Use comparison statements to arrange order

Same formula applies for height / weight / age / marks
