Direction Sense

Movement / Distance / Angle - SSC MTS Exam Section

Concept

In direction sense questions you track a person's movement on a map — North / South / East / West — and then find final direction, shortest distance from starting point, or angle turn (left/right) after movement.

Important:

These are very common and very scoring in SSC MTS. Always track facing direction before turning.

Exam Tip: Draw small rough diagrams to avoid confusion — it saves marks and time.

Basic Directions

↑

North

↓

South

→

East

←

West

Left / Right Turning Rules (Must Remember)

Facing North — Left = West, Right = East

Facing South — Left = East, Right = West

Facing East — Left = North, Right = South

Facing West — Left = South, Right = North

Distance Calculation

Pythagoras Theorem for Shortest Distance:

Distance = √(x² + y²)

Example: Walk 6 km North, then 8 km East

= √(6² + 8²) = √100 = 10 km

Common Triangles: 3-4-5, 6-8-10, 5-12-13

Memorize these for quick calculations

Solved Examples

A man is facing North. He turns right → East. Then right again → South. Then left → East.

Final direction? East

A man walks 6 km North, then 8 km East.

Shortest distance = √(6² + 8²) = √100 = 10 km

Facing West, turn left, walk, turn left again

You would now face East

Practice Questions

A person is facing East. He turns right, then right again, then left. Final direction?

Options: A) South B) East C) West D) North

Track facing direction step by step

View Answer

A) South

2

A person walks 5 km North, then 12 km West. Shortest distance from starting point?

Options: A) 13 km B) 10 km C) 14 km D) 17 km

Use Pythagoras theorem

View Answer

A) 13 km

3

Facing South, turn left → ?

Options: A) East B) West C) North D) South

Apply left/right rules based on facing direction

View Answer

A) East

4

A boy walks 7 km North, then 24 km East. How far from starting point?

Options: A) 25 km B) 26 km C) 23 km D) 27 km

Use Pythagoras: 7-24-25 triangle

View Answer

A) 25 km

5

Walk 3 km North, 4 km East, 12 km South → distance from start?

Options: A) 15 km B) 13 km C) 9 km D) 7 km

Calculate net displacement

View Answer

B) 13 km

Quick Recap

ALWAYS track facing direction before turning

Apply left/right rules based on facing direction

Distance questions mostly use Pythagorean triangles

Draw small diagrams to avoid confusion
