Mirror & Water Images

Reflection Concepts - SSC MTS Exam Section

Concept

These questions check how a figure/letter/number looks when reflected in a mirror (vertical reflection) or in water (horizontal reflection).

Mirror Image

Vertical reflection = Left ↔ Right reversal

Water Image

Horizontal reflection = Top ↔ Bottom reversal

Exam Tip: Remember the key difference - Mirror swaps left-right, Water swaps top-bottom.

Mirror Image (Vertical Reflection)

Right side becomes left side

Left side becomes right side

Top and bottom remain same

Example quick intuition:

If arrow → pointing right normally → mirror will show arrow pointing left

Letters that look same in mirror:

A, H, I, M, O, T, U, V, W, X, Y

Digits that look same:

0, 1, 8

Water Image (Horizontal Reflection)

Top becomes bottom

Bottom becomes top

Left and right remain same

Example:

Arrow ↑ becomes ↓ in water image

Key difference:

Water image is upside down, not sideways

Solved Examples

Mirror Image of PQ

→ becomes QP style reversed (left-right swap)

Water Image of A↑

→ A remains A but arrow becomes ↓ (top-bottom swap)

Mirror image of "2"

→ will look left reversed (curve orientation flips)

Practice Questions

1

Which letters remain SAME in mirror image?

Options: a) A and B b) H and X c) E and F d) K and P

Remember symmetrical letters

View Answer

b) H and X

Both H and X are symmetrical and look same in mirror

2

Mirror image of → arrow pointing right?

Options: a) left b) up c) down d) same

Mirror reverses left-right direction

View Answer

a) left

Mirror image: right → left (left-right reversal)

3

Water image of ↓ arrow will be

Options: a) left b) right c) up d) no change

Water reverses top-bottom direction

View Answer

c) up

Water image: ↓ → ↑ (top-bottom reversal)

4

Mirror image reverses which side?

Options: a) Top-bottom b) Left-right c) Both d) none

Remember the basic mirror property

View Answer

b) Left-right

Mirror image swaps left and right sides

5

Water image reverses which side?

Options: a) Left-right b) Top-bottom c) Both d) none

Remember the basic water reflection property

View Answer

b) Top-bottom

Water image swaps top and bottom sides

Quick Recap

Mirror → left-right swap

Water → top-bottom swap

Some letters stay same in mirror:

A H I M O T U V W X Y

Practice improves visual imagination speed
