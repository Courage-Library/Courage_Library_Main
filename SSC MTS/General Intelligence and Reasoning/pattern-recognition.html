Pattern Recognition

Hidden Rules & Transformations - SSC MTS Exam Section

Concept

In this type, you have to identify the hidden rule in figures / symbols / arrangement. The pattern may be based on shape repetition, symmetry, position movement, rotation, shading, or combination of multiple small changes happening together.

Key Idea:

Pattern recognition = find "why the next step looks like that".

Exam Tip: Look for consistent changes between consecutive figures. The pattern usually continues in the same manner.

What to Observe in Pattern Recognition

Observation Area Meaning
Shape repetition same shapes appearing in specific sequence
Symmetry vertical / horizontal / diagonal symmetry
Transformation shapes getting modified (add, remove, half, merge)
Rotation / Flip fixed direction rotation or mirror reflection
Shading style dark-light-dark pattern
Counting logic number of sides increasing or decreasing
Positional Logic shapes moving along fixed positions

Solved Examples (Short)

If in every next figure, the number of sides of polygon increases by 1

→ from triangle → square → pentagon → next will be hexagon

If the dark shaded block moves one place clockwise each step

→ next block position is one step ahead again

If shape splits into 2 parts, next step may combine back or rotate separately

→ observe the transformation pattern carefully

Practice Questions

1

A triangle becomes a square, square becomes pentagon, pentagon becomes hexagon. Next will be?

Options: a) 6 sides b) 7 sides c) 8 sides d) 9 sides

Count the sides pattern: 3→4→5→6→?

View Answer

b) 7 sides

Pattern: +1 side each step: 3→4→5→6→7

2

An arrow flips horizontally every step. After facing → right, next will face?

Options: a) up b) left c) down d) no change

Horizontal flip reverses left-right direction

View Answer

b) left

Horizontal flip: right → left → right → left

3

One element disappears every step. If last step had 3 elements, the next would have:

Options: a) 1 b) 2 c) 3 d) 4

-1 pattern continues consistently

View Answer

b) 2

-1 pattern: 4→3→2→1 (if started from 4)

4

2 dark → 1 dark → 2 dark → 1 dark… next?

Options: a) 2 dark b) no dark c) 1 dark d) cannot say

Alternate pattern continues

View Answer

a) 2 dark

Alternate pattern: 2→1→2→1→2

5

A dot moves in corners of square in CW direction. Last was at top left. Next will be at?

Options: a) top right b) bottom right c) bottom left d) same

CW movement: top left → top right → bottom right → bottom left

View Answer

a) top right

CW movement: top left → top right → bottom right → bottom left → top left

Quick Recap

Identify what changes every step

Count shapes / sides / blocks systematically

Rotation + shading mostly combine

Symmetry is one of the easiest pattern tests

Previous Year Based Questions

1

On each step, number of sides increases by 2 (4 → 6 → 8 → ?). Next shape sides =

Options: a) 10 b) 7 c) 9 d) 11

Year: 2020 | Shift: 1

View Answer

a) 10

+2 pattern: 4→6→8→10

2

Shaded square moves ACW at equal jumps. If current is bottom right, next is:

Options: a) top right b) bottom left c) top left d) between corners

Year: 2021 | Shift: 2

View Answer

b) bottom left

ACW: bottom right → bottom left → top left → top right

3

Symbol alternates between mirror left and mirror right. After right mirror, next will be:

Options: a) left mirror b) no mirror c) rotate shape d) dark shade

Year: 2022 | Shift: 1

View Answer

a) left mirror

Alternate pattern: left mirror → right mirror → left mirror → right mirror

Remember that patterns in SSC MTS are usually straightforward and continue consistently throughout the sequence.
