Blood Relations

SSC GD Exam

1. What are Blood Relations?

Blood Relations are logical reasoning questions that test your ability to identify family relationships between different people based on given information. These questions measure your analytical thinking and relationship mapping skills.

Family Relationship Mapping

Logical Deduction

Generation Analysis

Puzzle Solving

Simple Definition: Blood Relations involve identifying family connections and determining relationships between different family members.

4. Coded Blood Relations

Common Coding Patterns

Symbol Coding

+ = brother, - = sister

Mathematical Coding

A×B = A is brother of B

Letter Coding

F = Father, M = Mother

Mixed Coding

Combination of patterns

Coded Relations Examples

Code Meaning Example Interpretation
A + B A is brother of B X + Y X is brother of Y
A - B A is sister of B P - Q P is sister of Q
A × B A is father of B M × N M is father of N
A ÷ B A is mother of B C ÷ D C is mother of D

Generation Puzzle Examples

Puzzle Type Given Information Question Solution Approach
3-Generation A is B's father. B is C's mother. How is A related to C? A is grandfather of C
Mixed Generation P is Q's brother. Q is R's father. How is P related to R? P is uncle of R
Complex X is Y's husband. Y is Z's sister. How is X related to Z? X is brother-in-law of Z
Multi-level M is N's grandfather. N is O's mother. How is M related to O? M is great-grandfather of O

6. Family Tree Construction

Building Family Trees

Basic Symbols

□ = Male

Square represents male members

○ = Female

Circle represents female members

| = Parent-Child

Vertical line shows parent-child relationship

Relationship Lines

--- = Sibling

Horizontal line connects siblings

= = Marriage

Double line shows marriage relationship

+ = Generation

Plus indicates same generation level

Always start with the person whose relationship is clearly defined and build the tree step by step!
