SSC GD Self-Paced Course

1. What are Series?

Series are sequences of numbers, alphabets, or mixed elements that follow a specific pattern or rule. The task is to identify the pattern and find the missing or wrong element in the sequence.

Pattern Recognition

Logical Sequencing

Mathematical Operations

Predictive Analysis

Simple Definition: Series are ordered sequences where each element follows a specific rule from the previous one.

7. How to Solve Series Questions

Step-by-Step Approach:

1

Observe the Pattern

Look at differences between consecutive terms

2

Check Common Patterns

Arithmetic, geometric, squares, primes etc.

3

Verify the Pattern

Apply pattern to all terms to confirm

4

Find Missing/Wrong

Apply pattern to find solution

Always check multiple possibilities - sometimes there's more than one pattern!
