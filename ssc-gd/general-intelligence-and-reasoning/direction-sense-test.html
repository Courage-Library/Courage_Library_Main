Direction Sense Test

SSC GD Exam

1. What is Direction Sense Test?

Direction Sense Test is a logical reasoning exercise that tests your ability to visualize and track movement in different directions. It involves calculating final positions and directions after a series of movements and turns.

Spatial Awareness

Path Tracking

Distance Calculation

Direction Analysis

Simple Definition: Direction Sense involves tracking movement paths and determining final positions relative to starting points.

Movement Distance Displacement Explanation

M

Complex Path Examples

Starting Direction Turns Final Direction Explanation
North Right → Right → Left East N→E→S→E (Right+Right-Left=+90°)
East Left → Right → Right → Left West E→N→E→S→W (Net 180° turn)
South About turn → Left → Right South S→N→W→S (Back to original)
West Right → Left → Left → Right West W→N→W→S→W (Net 0° change)

6. Shadow Based Directions

Using Shadows to Find Directions

Morning (Sunrise)

Sun in East

Shadows fall West

If facing Sun

East - Front, West - Back

If shadow in front

Facing West

Evening (Sunset)

Sun in West

Shadows fall East

If facing Sun

West - Front, East - Back

If shadow in front

Facing East

Remember: Shadows are always opposite to the sun's position!

7. How to Solve Direction Sense Questions

Step-by-Step Approach:

1

Draw Diagram

Sketch the path with directions

2

Track Direction Changes

Note each turn and its effect

3

Calculate Coordinates

Use X-Y axis for complex paths

4

Verify Answer

Double-check with diagram

Always use the coordinate method for complex paths: North/South as Y-axis, East/West as X-axis!
