Classification (Odd One Out)

SSC GD Exam

1. What is Classification?

Classification is a logical reasoning exercise where you identify the odd one out from a group of items based on a common pattern or relationship. It tests your ability to recognize similarities and differences among given options.

Pattern Recognition

Logical Grouping

Analytical Thinking

Category Identification

Simple Definition: Classification means grouping similar items and identifying the one that doesn't belong to that group.

7. How to Solve Classification Questions

Step-by-Step Approach:

1

Observe All Options

Carefully examine all given items

2

Find Common Pattern

Identify what most items have in common

3

Identify the Mismatch

Find which item doesn't follow the pattern

4

Verify Your Answer

Double-check with all options

Always look for multiple possible patterns. The most obvious one is usually correct.
