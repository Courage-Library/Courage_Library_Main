SSC GD Exam - Quantitative Aptitude

1. What is Approximation?

Approximation means finding a value close to the exact value, which is good enough for practical purposes or for quick mental calculations.

Exam Context:
In exams like SSC, Delhi Police, or Banking, approximation helps you solve questions faster when an exact answer is not required.
