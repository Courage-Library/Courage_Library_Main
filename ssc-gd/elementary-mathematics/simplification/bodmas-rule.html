BODMAS stands for the order of operations used to solve mathematical expressions correctly. When multiple operations (like +, ‚Äì, √ó, √∑, brackets, etc.) occur together, we must follow a specific sequence.

Key Concept:
BODMAS ensures everyone solves mathematical expressions in the same way and gets the same correct answer.
