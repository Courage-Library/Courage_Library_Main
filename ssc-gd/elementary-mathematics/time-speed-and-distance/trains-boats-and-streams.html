Q1. A train 150 m long crosses a pole in 15 sec. Find its speed.

View Answer

Speed = 150/15 = 10 m/s = 36 km/h

Q2. A 300 m train crosses a 200 m platform in 25 sec. Find speed.

View Answer

Distance = 300 + 200 = 500 m, Speed = 500/25 = 20 m/s = 72 km/h

Q3. Train A (200 m) and Train B (300 m) run in opposite directions at 54 km/h and 36 km/h. Time to cross?

View Answer

Relative speed = 54+36 = 90 km/h = 25 m/s, Distance = 200+300 = 500 m, Time = 500/25 = 20 sec

Q4. Two trains (150 m & 250 m) in same direction at 60 km/h & 42 km/h. Crossing time?

View Answer

Relative speed = 18 km/h = 5 m/s, Distance = 150+250 = 400 m, Time = 400/5 = 80 sec

Q5. A boat travels 30 km downstream in 3 hours. Find downstream speed.

View Answer

Speed = 30/3 = 10 km/h

Q6. Upstream speed = 6 km/h, downstream speed = 10 km/h. Find boat speed.

View Answer

B = (6+10)/2 = 8 km/h

Q7. Upstream = 8 km/h, Stream speed = 2 km/h. Find boat speed.

View Answer

B = U + S = 8 + 2 = 10 km/h

Q8. Boat speed = 12 km/h, stream = 3 km/h. Find upstream speed.

View Answer

U = B ‚àí S = 12 ‚àí 3 = 9 km/h

Q9. A boat covers 20 km upstream in 4 hours. Find upstream speed.

View Answer

U = 20/4 = 5 km/h

Q10. A train running at 72 km/h crosses a pole in 10 sec. Find length.

View Answer

72 km/h = 20 m/s, Length = 20 √ó 10 = 200 m

Q11. A 240 m train crosses a man in 12 sec. Find speed.

View Answer

Q12. Upstream speed = 4 km/h, downstream = 16 km/h. Find stream speed.

Q13. A boat takes 2 hours for 20 km downstream. Find downstream speed.

View Answer

D = 20/2 = 10 km/h

Q14. If boat speed = 15 km/h and downstream speed = 20 km/h, find stream speed.

View Answer

S = D ‚àí B = 20 ‚àí 15 = 5 km/h

Q15. Train A (120 m) crosses Train B (180 m) in 12 sec. Relative speed = 25 m/s. Find direction.

View Answer

Distance = 300 m, Speed = 300/12 = 25 m/s, Matches given relative speed = opposite direction.
