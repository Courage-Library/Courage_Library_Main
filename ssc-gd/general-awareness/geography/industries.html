Industries in India

SSC GD Self-Paced Course

1. What is an Industry?

An industry is an economic activity related to:

  • Production of goods
  • Extraction of minerals
  • Manufacturing
  • Providing services

2. Types of Industries (Very Important)

1. Primary Industry

  • Related to natural resources
  • Example: Mining, Fishing, Agriculture

2. Secondary Industry

  • Manufacturing goods
  • Example: Steel, Textile, Cement

3. Tertiary Industry

  • Service-based
  • Example: Banking, Transport, Education

5. Cotton Textile Industry

Key Facts

  • Oldest industry in India
  • First cotton mill: Mumbai (1854)
  • Called: "Sunrise Industry"
  • Leading State: Gujarat

Why in Maharashtra & Gujarat?

  • Black soil (good for cotton)
  • Humid climate
  • Port facilities for export

6. Jute Industry

Key Information

  • First jute mill: Rishra (West Bengal)
  • Maximum mills in: West Bengal
  • Raw material: Jute

Reason for Concentration in WB

Ganga Delta region produces jute (ideal climatic conditions)
