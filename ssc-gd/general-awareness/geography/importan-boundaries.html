3. Important Boundary Lines (Extremely Important)

1. Radcliffe Line

  • Between: India & Pakistan
  • Year: 1947
  • Named after: Cyril Radcliffe
  • Formed during Partition

2. McMahon Line

  • Between: India & China
  • Drawn in: 1914
  • Named after: Henry McMahon
  • Separates Arunachal Pradesh from China
  • China does not officially accept it

3. Durand Line

  • Between: Pakistan & Afghanistan
  • India shares small border with Afghanistan via Ladakh region
  • Named after: Mortimer Durand

4. Maritime Boundary (Sea Border)

  • India – Sri Lanka separated by: Palk Strait
  • Southernmost Point of India: Indira Point
  • Located in: Andaman & Nicobar Islands
