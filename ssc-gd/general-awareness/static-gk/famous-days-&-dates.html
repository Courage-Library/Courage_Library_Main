Famous Days & Dates

Complete SSC GD Syllabus Coverage

Introduction

Questions on important days and dates are high-scoring and direct. SSC GD often asks specific dates of national/international days, themes of major days, and matching type questions.

This article covers all important days month-wise.

1. January – Important Days

January 1 – Global Family Day
January 9 – Pravasi Bharatiya Divas
January 12 – National Youth Day (Birth anniversary of Swami Vivekananda)
January 15 – Army Day
January 23 – Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose Jayanti
January 24 – National Girl Child Day
January 25 – National Voters Day, National Tourism Day
January 26 – Republic Day
January 30 – Martyrs' Day
