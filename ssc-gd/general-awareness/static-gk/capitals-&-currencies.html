12. Memory Tricks for SSC GD

  • Japan → Tokyo → Yen
    Three words → Same last letter (n)
  • Countries using Rupee:
    India, Pakistan, Nepal, Sri Lanka, Indonesia (Rupiah)
  • Euro Countries Trick:
    Most Western & Central Europe = Euro
  • Dollar Trick:
    If unsure, many island nations + US use dollar
