Important Books & Authors

Complete SSC GD Syllabus Coverage

Introduction

Books and authors are a high-scoring static GK topic. SSC GD frequently asks about names of books, authors, books written by famous personalities, autobiographies, and award-winning books.

This article covers all important books & authors required for SSC GD and other government exams.
