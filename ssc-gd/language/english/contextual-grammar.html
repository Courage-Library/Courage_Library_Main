Contextual Grammar

SSC GD Self-Paced Course

Introduction

Contextual Grammar questions test how well you understand the sentence, not just grammar rules. You must choose the option that fits meaning, grammar, tone, and logic.

Meaning (context)

Grammar (correct form)

Tone (positive/negative)

Logic (what makes sense)

Exam Tip: SSC GD keeps this EASY. Most mistakes happen because students don't read the sentence fully.

What Is Contextual Grammar?

Choosing the correct word or phrase based on what the sentence is talking about, what meaning is needed, and what grammar structure fits.

Example:

He completed the work ____ time.

in / on / at / by

✔ on time

(because the expression is fixed)

Types of Contextual Grammar Questions

1. Choose the Word That Makes Sense

Sentence will give a clue about meaning.

Example: She is very ____ and always helps others.

selfish / kind / angry / rude

✔ kind

2. Choose the Correct Preposition

Some prepositions are used ONLY in certain phrases.

fond ___ → of

good ___ → at

interested ___ → in

3. Choose the Correct Verb Form

Look at tense and context.

Example: He ____ here since morning.

a) is b) was c) has been d) will be

✔ has been (since = perfect tense)

4. Choose the Correct Conjunction

Understand meaning of the sentence.

Example: He worked hard ____ he failed.

a) but b) because c) so d) and

✔ but (contrast)

5. Choose the Correct Article

a / an / the will depend on context.

Example: He is ___ honest man.

✔ an

6. Choose the Correct Pronoun

Context decides the correct pronoun.

Everyone did ____ best.

his/her / their / his / our

✔ his/her (SSC prefers singular)

Context Clues to Look For

These are small hints that tell the correct answer:

✔ "Because, due to" → reason

✔ "But, although" → contrast

✔ "Therefore, so" → result

✔ "Since, for" → time

✔ "On, in, at" → place/time rules

✔ Tone → positive or negative word?

Example Sentences (SSC GD Level)

He is interested ___ music.

✔ in

Riya is very ____; everyone likes her.

✔ polite

He did not go to school ____ he was ill.

✔ because

This is ____ best day of my life.

✔ the

She has been working here ____ two years.

✔ for

He succeeded ____ his hard work.

✔ through / by

Sample Questions (SSC GD Style)

1

Choose the correct word: He was tired, ____ he continued working.

Options: (a) but (b) because (c) although (d) so

View Answer

but

(contrast)

2

Choose the correct preposition: She is afraid ___ dogs.

Options: (a) of (b) from (c) in (d) by

View Answer

of

3

Choose the word that fits the meaning: He is very ____ and never tells a lie.

Options: (a) honest (b) rich (c) rude (d) short

View Answer

honest

4

Choose the correct verb: The show ____ at 7 PM every day.

Options: (a) start (b) starts (c) started (d) starting

View Answer

starts

(simple present habit)

5

Fill the blank keeping tone in mind: The weather is ____ today.

Options: (a) delicious (b) beautiful (c) dangerous (d) bitter

View Answer

beautiful

Quick Recap

Context decides the answer

Look for tone → positive/negative

Prepositions matter: fond of, good at, interested in

Read full sentence before marking

Logic > grammar sometimes

SSC GD usually tests:

✔ prepositions
✔ conjunctions
✔ verb forms
✔ adjectives based on tone
