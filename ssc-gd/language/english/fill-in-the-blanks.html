Fill in the Blanks

SSC GD Self-Paced Course

Introduction

Fill in the Blanks is a super easy & high-scoring topic in SSC GD. Sentences are very simple, and the blank is usually filled by basic grammar and vocabulary.

Basic vocabulary

Correct preposition

Correct article

Correct tense

Correct conjunction

Simple grammar logic

Exam Tip: If you know basic English, this topic becomes full marks.

Types of Fill in the Blanks in SSC GD

1. Vocabulary-Based

You choose the word that fits the meaning.

Example: He is very ____ at maths.

good / bad / tall / heavy

✔ good

2. Grammar-Based

Correct form of article, verb, pronoun, preposition, etc.

Example: She ___ going to school.

is / are / be / am

✔ is

3. Context-Based

Choose the word that matches the sentence meaning.

Example: Rahul always speaks the ____.

truth / lie / noise / mistake

✔ truth

How to Solve Quickly

✔ Read the whole sentence

Sometimes the answer depends on context.

✔ Look at the words before and after the blank

They give hints.

✔ Eliminate wrong options

Usually 2 options are totally wrong → delete them.

✔ Apply basic grammar rules

a/an/the, is/are, in/on/at, because/but/so

✔ Understand tone

positive, negative, contrast, result, reason.

High-Frequency Words Asked in SSC GD

SSC GD Style Practice Examples

Vocabulary-Type

1

He is ____ honest man.

Options: (a) a (b) an (c) the (d) no article

View Answer

an

(an honest → h silent)

2

She is very ____ in English.

Options: (a) good (b) tall (c) big (d) short

View Answer

good

3

Please ____ me a glass of water.

Options: (a) bring (b) sings (c) walks (d) opens

View Answer

bring

Preposition-Type

1

The book is ____ the table.

Options: (a) in (b) on (c) at (d) under

View Answer

on

2

He is fond ____ music.

Options: (a) of (b) to (c) for (d) in

View Answer

of

Verb/Tense-Type

1

She ____ to school every day.

Options: (a) go (b) goes (c) going (d) gone

View Answer

goes

2

They ____ playing cricket.

Options: (a) is (b) are (c) was (d) be

View Answer

are

Conjunction-Type

1

I tried hard ____ I failed.

Options: (a) and (b) but (c) or (d) because

View Answer

but

2

She went to school ____ it was raining.

Options: (a) although (b) because (c) and (d) or

View Answer

although

Context-Type

1

He always speaks the ____.

Options: (a) truth (b) mistake (c) sound (d) problem

View Answer

truth

2

Hard work leads to ____.

Options: (a) success (b) water (c) noise (d) mistake

View Answer

success

Quick Recap

Understand the context

Check grammar around the blank

Articles: a/an/the

Prepositions: at/in/on/of/to

Verbs: go/goes, is/are

Conjunctions: because, but, although

Eliminate wrong options

SSC GD uses simple words, nothing difficult
