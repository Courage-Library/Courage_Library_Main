Subject–Verb Agreement

English Grammar - SSC GD Preparation

Introduction

Subject–Verb Agreement means the verb must match the subject in number (singular/plural) and person.

Singular Subject → Singular Verb

He plays. The boy runs.

Plural Subject → Plural Verb

They play. The boys run.

Exam Tip: SSC GD asks very simple SVA questions. If you learn these rules → 2–3 marks free.

Most Important SSC GD Rules

1. He / She / It / Singular Noun → Verb + s/es

He writes

She goes

It works

The dog barks

2. I / You / We / They → Verb (base form)

I eat

You play

We work

They study

3. "And" makes the subject plural

Ram and Shyam are friends.

The cat and the dog are playing.

4. "Either…or" / "Neither…nor" → Verb follows the nearest subject

Either Ram or his friends are coming.

Neither he nor I am ready.

Either you or he is guilty.

5. Words like: Everyone, Someone, Each, Every → Singular verb

Everyone is

Each student was

Every girl plays

Someone has

6. Collective nouns → Usually Singular

Team is

Family is

Army is

(Unless the meaning is plural → "The team are fighting among themselves." But SSC won't ask this tricky version.)

7. Distance, Time, Money → Singular

Five kilometers is a long distance.

Ten rupees is not enough.

Two hours is remaining.

8. Plural-looking words but Singular meaning

News is

Mathematics is

Politics is

9. "One of the + plural noun" → Verb is singular

One of the boys is missing.

One of the cars was stolen.

10. Noun after preposition doesn't matter

The quality of the apples is good.

(Subject = "quality" → singular)

The color of the flowers is nice.

(Subject = "color" → singular)

Common SSC GD Mistakes (Very High-Frequency)

❌ He do his work.

✔ He does his work.

❌ They is playing.

✔ They are playing.

❌ One of the girl are absent.

✔ One of the girls is absent.

❌ Each students are present.

✔ Each student is present.

Examples (SSC GD Level)

The teacher teaches English.

My friends are coming.

The list of items is long.

Neither of them was late.

Ten kilometers is far.

Everyone knows the answer.

Sample Questions (SSC GD Style)

1

Choose the correct option: Each of the boys ___ present.

Options: (a) are (b) were (c) is (d) be

View Answer

is

2

Choose the correct form: The players and the coach ___ tired.

Options: (a) is (b) are (c) was (d) be

View Answer

are

3

Fill in the blank: The price of vegetables ___ rising.

Options: (a) are (b) were (c) is (d) have

View Answer

is

(Subject = price → singular)

4

Pick the correct sentence:

Options: (a) One of the boys are absent (b) One of the boys is absent (c) One of the boy is absent (d) One of boy are absent

View Answer

One of the boys is absent

5

Correct option: Neither Ram nor his friends ___ late.

Options: (a) is (b) are (c) was (d) be

View Answer

are

(Nearest subject = friends → plural → "are")

Quick Recap

He/She/It = verb + s/es

I/You/They = base verb

"And" → plural

"Either…or / Neither…nor" → verb follows nearest subject

Each/Every/Everyone → singular verb

Distances, money, time → singular

One of the + plural noun → singular verb

Collective nouns → usually singular

Key Point: SSC GD uses only these rules.
