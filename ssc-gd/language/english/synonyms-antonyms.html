Synonyms & Antonyms

SSC GD English Section

Introduction

Synonyms and Antonyms are one of the easiest scoring areas in SSC GD English. Questions are direct, simple and mostly from basic vocabulary.

Synonyms

Words with similar meanings

Antonyms

Words with opposite meanings

Exam Tip: SSC usually asks 2-4 questions from this topic.

Synonyms

Meaning

Words that have the same or nearly the same meaning.

Happy → Glad

Quick → Fast

How to learn synonyms easily

Learn words in groups - Group related words together

Focus on tone - Identify positive/negative connotations

Create small pairs - Strong → Powerful, Weak → Feeble

Important Synonyms for SSC GD

Word Synonym
Abandon Leave
Aid Help
Brief Short
Cautious Alert
Dense Thick
Enemy Opponent
Genuine Real
Harsh Severe
Ignorant Unaware
Yield Produce

Antonyms

Meaning

Words that have opposite meanings.

Hot ↔ Cold

Increase ↔ Decrease

How to identify antonyms

Look for negative prefixes - un-, in-, dis-

Understand the degree - Huge ↔ Tiny

Check context - Words with multiple meanings

Important Antonyms for SSC GD

Bold Timid

Quick Recap

Synonym = similar meaning

Antonym = opposite meaning

Mostly basic words are asked in exams

Pair-learning helps: Harsh → Severe / Soft

SSC repeats words like: Scarcity, Victory, Ancient

Practice regularly with previous year questions
