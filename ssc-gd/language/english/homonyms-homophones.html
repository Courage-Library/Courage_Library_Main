Homonyms & Homophones

SSC GD English Section

Introduction

Homonyms and Homophones look confusing, but SSC GD asks only very basic, everyday words. These questions come mostly from spelling and sound similarity.

Homonyms

Meaning

Homonyms are words that have the same spelling or same pronunciation, but different meanings.

Bat

a flying animal

a cricket bat

Bank

a financial institution

the side of a river

Important Homonyms for SSC GD

Word Meaning 1 Meaning 2
Right correct opposite of left
Light not heavy brightness
Match a game something that makes fire
Fair honest a festival/exhibition
Kind type generous
Letter character in alphabet written message

Exam Tip: These are enough for SSC-level homonym understanding.

Homophones

Meaning

Homophones are words that sound the same but have different spellings and different meanings.

Sea (ocean)

See (to look)

Note: These are frequently asked in SSC exams.

Most Important Homophones for SSC GD

Word 1 Meaning Word 2 Meaning
Accept agree Except excluding
Allowed permitted Aloud loudly
Weak not strong Week 7 days
Peace calm Piece portion
Brake stopping device Break to shatter
Write to pen down Right correct / direction
Mail letter Male opposite of female
Here this place Hear to listen
Some a few Sum total
Stationary not moving Stationery writing materials
Hole empty space Whole complete
Flour powder used to cook Flower blossom
Knight soldier Night opposite of day
Weather climate Whether if
Dual twofold Duel fight

Note: These are high-frequency exam words.

Easy Tricks to Remember

Context in Sentences

"I can hear music" vs. "Come here"

Visual Memory

Stationery → 'e' = pens, paper

Practice Pairs

Piece / Peace, Allowed / Aloud

Sample Questions (SSC GD Style)

1

Choose the correct word: I will ______ you tomorrow.

Options: (a) meet (b) meat

View Answer

meet

2

Choose the correct pair of homophones:

Options: (a) Sun – Son (b) Book – Buck (c) Great – Create (d) Chair – Share

View Answer

Sun – Son

3

Fill in the blank: She bought a ______ of cake.

Options: (a) peace (b) piece

View Answer

piece

4

Choose the correct word: The weather is pleasant today.

Options: (a) weather (b) whether

View Answer

weather

5

Identify the homophone of "flower."

Options: (a) flour (b) flur (c) flowr (d) flar

View Answer

flour

Quick Recap

Homonyms = same spelling or sound, different meanings

Homophones = same sound, different spelling + meaning

SSC GD mostly asks homophones in exams

Most repeated: Accept/Except, Piece/Peace

High frequency: Brake/Break, Flour/Flower

Important pairs: Weather/Whether, Mail/Male
