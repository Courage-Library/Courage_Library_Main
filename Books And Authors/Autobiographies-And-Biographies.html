Autobiographies & Biographies

Reference: Lucent GK, NCERT Class 6–12

1. Popular Autobiographies & Biographies

Book Title Author / Subject Key Details / Theme
Playing It My Way Sachin Tendulkar (with Boria Majumdar) Autobiography of the cricket legend; covers childhood to retirement
Unbreakable Mary Kom Inspiring story of the Indian boxer's struggles and victories
The Race of My Life Milkha Singh (with Sonia Sanwalka) Life story of "The Flying Sikh"; partition, athletics, resilience
The Test of My Life Yuvraj Singh Focuses on his fight against cancer and return to cricket
Open Andre Agassi International bestseller; deep personal insights into tennis life
I Am Malala Malala Yousafzai (with Christina Lamb) Story of her fight for girls' education in Pakistan under Taliban rule

SSC Tips:

  • Sachin Tendulkar → Playing It My Way
  • Mary Kom → Unbreakable
  • Milkha Singh → The Race of My Life
  • Malala Yousafzai → I Am Malala
