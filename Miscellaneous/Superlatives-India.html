Superlatives – India

Reference: Lucent GK, NCERT Class 6–12

1. Geography & States

Superlative Answer
Largest State (Area) Rajasthan
Smallest State (Area) Goa
Most Populous State Uttar Pradesh
Least Populous State Sikkim
Densest State (Population Density) Bihar
Least Dense State Arunachal Pradesh
Largest Union Territory (Area) Ladakh
Smallest Union Territory (Area) Lakshadweep
First Fully Organic State Sikkim
Cleanest State (Swachh Survekshan 2023) Madhya Pradesh (Large state category)

2. Water Bodies & Landforms

Superlative Answer
Longest River in India (within boundary) Ganga
Longest River (including tributaries) Indus
Largest River Island Majuli (Assam)
Largest Delta Sundarbans (Ganga–Brahmaputra delta)
Largest Lake (Freshwater) Wular Lake (Jammu & Kashmir)
Largest Lake (Overall) Vembanad Lake (Kerala)
Highest Waterfall Kunchikal Falls (Karnataka)
Largest Saltwater Lake Chilika Lake (Odisha)
Longest Dam Hirakud Dam (Odisha)
Highest Dam Tehri Dam (Uttarakhand)
Largest Dam (Storage) Bhakra Nangal Dam

3. Mountains & Peaks

Superlative Answer
Highest Peak in India (Overall) K2 (Godwin-Austen, in PoK)
Highest Peak in Indian Territory Kangchenjunga (Sikkim) – 8,586 m
Southernmost Peak Anamudi (Kerala) – Highest in Western Ghats
Highest Volcano (Active, Indian Territory) Barren Island (Andaman & Nicobar Islands)

4. Infrastructure

Superlative Answer
Longest Railway Platform Hubballi Station (Karnataka) – 1,507 meters
Longest Road Tunnel Atal Tunnel (Himachal Pradesh) – 9.02 km
Longest Railway Bridge Vembanad Rail Bridge (Kerala)
Longest Road Bridge Bhupen Hazarika Setu (Assam)
Largest Port (By Container Volume) Jawaharlal Nehru Port (Navi Mumbai)
Oldest Port Lothal (Ancient port, Indus Valley Civilization)
Longest Expressway (Operational) Delhi–Mumbai Expressway (partial, ~1,386 km)
Largest Bus Terminal Chennai Mofussil Bus Terminal (Tamil Nadu)

5. Education & Research

Superlative Answer
Oldest University Nalanda University (Ancient)
Oldest Modern University (Post-British) University of Calcutta (1857)
Largest University (Enrollment) Indira Gandhi National Open University (IGNOU)
Highest Literacy Rate (State) Kerala
Lowest Literacy Rate (State) Bihar

6. Government & Politics

Superlative Answer
First President of India Dr. Rajendra Prasad
First Prime Minister Pt. Jawaharlal Nehru
First Woman President Pratibha Patil
First Woman Prime Minister Indira Gandhi
First Governor General (Indian) C. Rajagopalachari
Youngest Prime Minister Rajiv Gandhi (Age 40)

7. Arts, Culture & Media

Superlative Answer
Largest Theatre (Seating Capacity) Raj Mandir Theatre, Jaipur
First Indian Talkie Film Alam Ara (1931)
First Indian Colour Film Kisan Kanya (1937)
First Film Submitted to Oscars Mother India (1957)

8. Defence

Superlative Answer
Largest Indian Army Command Northern Command
Largest Naval Base INS Kadamba (Karwar, Karnataka)
First Aircraft Carrier INS Vikrant (1961 – now decommissioned)
First Indigenous Aircraft Carrier INS Vikrant (2022 – IAC-1)
Highest Battlefield Siachen Glacier

9. Science & Space

Superlative Answer
First Satellite Aryabhata (1975)
First Mission to Moon Chandrayaan-1 (2008)
First Mission to Mars Mangalyaan (2013)
First Woman in Space from India Kalpana Chawla
First Solar Mission Aditya-L1 (2023)
First Private Rocket Launch Vikram-S by Skyroot Aerospace (2022)
