Preparing for IBPS PO 2025? This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know about the exam pattern, syllabus, eligibility, and preparation strategy to crack the exam with confidence.

The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) conducts the Probationary Officer (PO) exam every year to recruit candidates for public sector banks in India. With lakhs of aspirants appearing annually, having the right preparation strategy is the key to success.

IBPS PO 2025 Exam Highlights

Exam Name

IBPS PO (Probationary Officer)

Organizing Body

Institute of Banking Personnel Selection

Exam Mode

Online (Computer-Based Test)

Selection Process

Prelims → Mains → Interview

Participating Banks

11 Public Sector Banks (like PNB, Canara Bank, Bank of Baroda, etc.)

IBPS PO 2025 Eligibility Criteria

Before applying, make sure you fulfill the basic requirements:

Nationality

Must be a citizen of India

Educational Qualification

Graduation in any discipline from a recognized university

Age Limit

Minimum: 20 years, Maximum: 30 years (with relaxations)

IBPS PO 2025 Exam Pattern

The IBPS PO exam is conducted in three stages:

1 Preliminary Exam (Objective)

Subjects & Marks:

  • English Language – 30 questions (30 marks)
  • Quantitative Aptitude – 35 questions (35 marks)
  • Reasoning Ability – 35 questions (35 marks)

Exam Details:

  • Total: 100 questions, 100 marks
  • Duration: 60 minutes
  • Negative Marking: 0.25 marks per wrong answer

2 Mains Exam (Objective + Descriptive)

Objective Test:

  • Reasoning & Computer Aptitude – 45 questions (60 marks)
  • General/Economy/Banking Awareness – 40 questions (40 marks)
  • English Language – 35 questions (40 marks)
  • Data Analysis & Interpretation – 35 questions (60 marks)

Descriptive Test:

  • Descriptive Test – 2 questions (Essay & Letter, 25 marks)
  • Duration: 3 hours + 30 minutes (Descriptive)
  • Total Marks: 200 + 25 = 225 marks

3 Interview

Process:

  • Conducted by participating banks
  • Weightage: 100 marks
  • Usually 15-20 minutes duration

Final Selection:

  • Based on: Mains (80%) + Interview (20%)
  • Category-wise merit list
  • Document verification

IBPS PO 2025 Preparation Strategy

Understand the Exam First

Know the exam pattern, sectional timing, and negative marking.

Build Strong Basics

Start with NCERT (6th–10th) for Quant & Grammar books for English.

Practice with Mock Tests

Attempt IBPS PO previous year papers and full-length mocks regularly.

Focus on Weak Areas

Identify low-scoring sections and dedicate extra time.

GK & Current Affairs

Read daily news, banking awareness updates, and monthly magazines.

Time Management

Prelims: Don't get stuck on one question. Mains: Prioritize DI & Reasoning.

Final Tips for IBPS PO 2025

  • Start early, at least 6–8 months before the exam.
  • Balance between Prelims speed and Mains depth.
  • Keep a revision notebook for formulas, shortcuts, and current affairs.
  • Stay consistent, motivated, and track your progress weekly.
  • Take care of your health with proper sleep, diet, and exercise.
  • Join online communities for doubt clearance and motivation.

Conclusion

The IBPS PO 2025 exam is highly competitive, but with the right preparation strategy, dedication, and resources, you can secure your dream job in a public sector bank.
