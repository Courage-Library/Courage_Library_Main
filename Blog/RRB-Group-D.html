RRB Group D 2025 Complete Guide

Everything you need to know about exam pattern, syllabus, eligibility, and preparation strategy.

If you are planning to appear for the RRB Group D 2025 exam, this guide covers the exam pattern, syllabus, eligibility, preparation tips, and best books to help you crack the exam with confidence.

The Railway Recruitment Board (RRB) conducts the Group D exam to recruit candidates for various posts in Level 1 of the 7th CPC pay matrix, such as Track Maintainer, Helper, Assistant Pointsman, Porter, and Hospital Attendant. With lakhs of candidates applying every year, having a well-structured preparation strategy is essential.

RRB Group D 2025 Exam Highlights

Exam Name

RRB Group D (Level 1)

Conducting Body

Railway Recruitment Board

Exam Mode

Computer-Based Test (CBT)

Posts Offered

Track Maintainer, Helper, Assistant Pointsman, etc.

Pay Scale

Level 1 (Rs. 18,000 basic + allowances)

Selection Stages

CBT → PET → Document Verification → Medical Exam

RRB Group D 2025 Eligibility Criteria

Nationality

Candidate must be an Indian citizen.

Educational Qualification

Must have passed 10th / ITI / Diploma from a recognized board or institution.

Age Limit

  • General: 18 – 33 years
  • OBC: 18 – 36 years
  • SC/ST: 18 – 38 years

RRB Group D 2025 Exam Pattern

Computer-Based Test (CBT)

Subject Questions Marks
General Science 25 25
Mathematics 25 25
General Intelligence & Reasoning 30 30
General Awareness & Current Affairs 20 20
Total 100 100

Duration: 90 minutes

Negative Marking: 0.33 marks for each wrong answer

Physical Efficiency Test (PET)

Male Candidates

  • Run 1000 meters in 4 minutes 15 seconds
  • Lift and carry 35 kg for 100 meters in 2 minutes

Female Candidates

  • Run 1000 meters in 5 minutes 40 seconds
  • Lift and carry 20 kg for 100 meters in 2 minutes

Final Stages

Document Verification

Verification of all educational and personal documents for shortlisted candidates.

Medical Examination

Final medical check to ensure fitness for railway service.

RRB Group D 2025 Syllabus

General Science

  • Physics, Chemistry, Biology (10th level)
  • Basics of everyday science
  • Human body and health
  • Environmental science

Mathematics

  • Number System
  • Decimals & Fractions
  • LCM, HCF
  • Ratio & Proportion
  • Percentage, Profit & Loss, SI & CI
  • Time & Work, Time & Distance
  • Mensuration
  • Data Interpretation

General Intelligence & Reasoning

  • Coding-Decoding
  • Blood Relations
  • Analogy
  • Venn Diagrams
  • Series
  • Syllogism
  • Mathematical Operations
  • Puzzle & Seating Arrangement

General Awareness & Current Affairs

  • Current events (last 6–8 months)
  • Indian History, Polity, Geography, Economy
  • Railway-related news
  • Sports, Books & Authors, Awards
  • Important dates and events
  • Science and technology developments

Best Books for RRB Group D 2025

Mathematics

  • Quantitative Aptitude by R.S. Aggarwal – Comprehensive coverage of all math topics
  • Fast Track Objective Arithmetic by Rajesh Verma – Quick revision and practice

General Science

  • NCERT Books (Class 6–10 Science) – Build strong fundamentals
  • Lucent's General Science – Comprehensive coverage in simple language

Reasoning

  • A Modern Approach to Verbal & Non-Verbal Reasoning by R.S. Aggarwal – Complete reasoning guide
  • Analytical Reasoning by M.K. Pandey – Advanced practice problems

General Awareness

  • Lucent's GK – Comprehensive static GK coverage
  • Manorama Yearbook – Current affairs and general knowledge
  • Monthly Current Affairs PDFs – Stay updated with latest events

RRB Group D 2025 Preparation Strategy

1

Understand the Pattern First

Know which topics carry more weightage and prioritize accordingly. Focus more on Reasoning and Mathematics as they constitute 55% of the exam.

2

Build Basics from NCERT

Revise Class 6–10 NCERTs for Science and Maths before moving to advanced practice. This will strengthen your foundation.

3

Daily GK & Current Affairs

Spend at least 30 minutes daily on newspapers and current affairs magazines. Make notes of important events and revise them weekly.

4

Practice Reasoning & Maths Daily

Set a target of 20–30 questions per topic every day. Focus on accuracy first, then gradually increase your speed.

5

Mock Tests are Key

Attempt full-length mock tests to improve speed and accuracy. Analyze your performance after each test and work on weak areas.

6

Focus on PET Preparation

Alongside CBT, maintain physical fitness as PET is qualifying in nature. Start practicing running and weight training well in advance.

Conclusion

The RRB Group D 2025 exam is a golden opportunity for candidates aspiring to work in the Indian Railways. With a mix of basic knowledge, regular practice, current affairs updates, and physical fitness, you can secure your dream railway job.
