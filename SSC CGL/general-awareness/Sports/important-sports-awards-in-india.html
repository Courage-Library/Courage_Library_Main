Important Sports Awards in India

Reference: Lucent GK, NCERT Class 6–12

Major National Sports Awards

These are conferred annually by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports, Government of India.

Award Name Purpose Eligibility/ Highlights
Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award Highest sporting honour; given for most outstanding performance over 4 years Renamed in 2021; Earlier known as Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna
Arjuna Award For consistent excellent performance in sports over 4 years Recognizes national & international achievements
Dronacharya Award For excellence in coaching Includes lifetime & regular category
Dhyan Chand Award Lifetime achievement award in sports For former players with significant contribution

Note: These awards are announced every year on 29 August (National Sports Day – Dhyan Chand’s birth anniversary).

Key Features & Symbols of Awards

Award Named After Introduced In Given For
Khel Ratna Major Dhyan Chand 1991–92 Highest performance
Arjuna Award Arjuna (Mahabharata) 1961 Consistent excellence
Dronacharya Award Guru Dronacharya 1985 Coaching excellence
Dhyan Chand Award Major Dhyan Chand 2002 Lifetime achievement
