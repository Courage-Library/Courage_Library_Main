India in International Sports

Reference: Lucent GK, NCERT Class 6–12

1. Olympic Medal Winners (Recent – Tokyo 2020)

India won a total of 7 medals at the Tokyo Olympics (2020), the highest ever in a single edition.

Athlete Event Medal
Neeraj Chopra Javelin Throw Gold (1st ever in Athletics for India)
Mirabai Chanu Weightlifting (49 kg) Silver
Ravi Kumar Dahiya Wrestling (57 kg) Silver
PV Sindhu Badminton (Women’s Singles) Bronze
Lovlina Borgohain Boxing (69 kg) Bronze
Bajrang Punia Wrestling (65 kg) Bronze
Indian Men's Hockey Team Team Event Bronze (1st since 1980)

SSC Tip: Neeraj Chopra = First athletics gold for India

2. Historic Firsts (India at the Olympics)

Achievement Athlete Year
First Olympic Medal (Individual) K.D. Jadhav (Wrestling) 1952 (Helsinki)
First Olympic Gold (Individual) Abhinav Bindra (Shooting) 2008 (Beijing)
First Indian Woman Medalist Karnam Malleswari (Weightlifting) 2000 (Sydney)
First Woman Olympic Silver PV Sindhu (Badminton) 2016 (Rio)
First Woman Olympic Gold Not yet achieved as of 2024

SSC Tip: Abhinav Bindra = Shooting = First Individual Gold

3. Recent Achievements (2022–2024)

Thomas Cup Victory – 2022

  • India won the Thomas Cup (Men's Badminton Team Championship) for the first time in history
  • Beat Indonesia 3–0 in the final
  • Key players: Lakshya Sen, Kidambi Srikanth, Satwik-Chirag pair

World Athletics Championships (2023)

  • Neeraj Chopra won Gold in Javelin Throw (First Indian to win gold at World Athletics)
  • Silver Medalist in 2022

Chess Achievements

  • R Praggnanandhaa was runner-up at FIDE World Cup 2023
  • D Gukesh surpassed Viswanathan Anand to become India's No. 1 ranked chess player in 2024

Cricket (2023–24 Records)

  • ODI World Cup 2023: India reached the final but lost to Australia
  • Virat Kohli became first player to score 50 ODI centuries
  • Rohit Sharma set record for most World Cup sixes.
  • India won the T20 series against South Africa and Australia in 2023–24.
  • Indian Women's Team: Won the Asian Games 2023 Gold in T20 Cricket

SSC Tips:

  • Thomas Cup = First Win = 2022
  • World Athletics Gold = Neeraj Chopra
  • ODI 50 centuries = Virat Kohli
