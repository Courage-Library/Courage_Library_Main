Recent Government Schemes (Dynamic – Last 2 Years)

Reference: Lucent GK, NCERT Class 6–12

PM Vishwakarma Scheme (2023)

Feature Details
Objective Support traditional artisans and craftspeople (Vishwakarmas)
Beneficiaries 18 traditional trades (e.g., carpenter, blacksmith, potter, cobbler, tailor, goldsmith, etc.)
Benefits Skill training, toolkit incentive, collateral-free loans, digital empowerment
Loan Facility ₹1 lakh in 1st tranche; ₹2 lakh in 2nd tranche at concessional interest
Ministry Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME)
Launch Year 2023

PM Gati Shakti National Master Plan

Feature Details
Objective Integrated infrastructure development for multi-modal connectivity
Focus Roads, railways, ports, airports, logistics, industrial corridors
Tool GIS-based digital platform for coordinated project planning
Aim Cut logistics cost and enhance infrastructure efficiency
Ministry Ministry of Commerce & Industry
Launch Year 2021

National Hydrogen Mission

Feature Details
Objective Promote green hydrogen production and make India energy independent
Focus Clean energy transition, decarbonization, export potential
Target Produce 5 million metric tonnes of green hydrogen by 2030
Applications Refining, steel, mobility, shipping, power
Ministry Ministry of New and Renewable Energy
Launch Year 2021 (announced on Independence Day)

Ayushman Bharat Digital Health Mission (ABDHM)

Feature Details
Objective Create a digital health ecosystem and unique Health ID for every citizen
Features Personal health records, hospital registries, doctor and insurance registries
Benefit Access to health services, portability, and data security
Platform Health ID, DigiDoctor, Health Facility Registry (HFR)
Ministry Ministry of Health & Family Welfare
Launch Year Nationwide roll-out in 2021

Agnipath Scheme

Feature Details
Objective Short-term recruitment in Indian Armed Forces as “Agniveers”
Duration of Service 4 years (with exit package & future benefits)
Eligibility Youth aged 17.5 to 21 years
Retention 25% of Agniveers may be absorbed in regular cadre after 4 years
Forces Covered Army, Navy, and Air Force
Launched By Ministry of Defence
Launch Year 2022

PM eVIDYA

Feature Details
Objective Provide digital education during and beyond the COVID-19 pandemic
Key Components DIKSHA platform, SWAYAM MOOCs, Radio/TV/QR-code textbooks
TV Channel One dedicated channel (Swayam Prabha) for each grade (Class 1–12)
Focus Accessible, multi-modal digital learning
Ministry Ministry of Education
Launch Year 2020

One Nation One Ration Card (ONORC)

Feature Details
Objective Enable nationwide portability of ration cards for food security
Benefit Allows migrant beneficiaries to receive subsidized food grains anywhere in India
Coverage All 36 States/UTs (100% coverage achieved in 2022)
Integrated With Aadhaar, EPOS (Electronic Point of Sale)
Implemented By Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food & Public Distribution
Launch Year Phased rollout began in 2019; fully implemented by 2022
