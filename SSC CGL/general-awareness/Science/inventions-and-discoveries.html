Inventions and Discoveries

Physics

Invention / Discovery Inventor / Discoverer Remarks
Laws of Motion Isaac Newton Formulated 3 fundamental laws
Law of Gravitation Isaac Newton Universal law of attraction
Electromagnetism James Clerk Maxwell Unified electricity & magnetism
Electromagnetic Induction Michael Faraday Basis of electric generator
Photoelectric Effect Albert Einstein Nobel Prize 1921
X-rays Wilhelm Roentgen First Nobel in Physics (1901)
Radioactivity Henri Becquerel Natural radioactivity
Thermometer Galileo Galilei Early version
Light Speed Measurement Ole Rømer First to estimate speed of light
Electric Bulb Thomas Edison Improved practical design

Chemistry

Discovery / Invention Discoverer Remarks
Periodic Table Dmitri Mendeleev Based on atomic weight
Modern Atomic Theory John Dalton Proposed atoms as indivisible
Electron J.J. Thomson Negative particle in atom
Proton Ernest Rutherford Positive particle in nucleus
Neutron James Chadwick Neutral particle
Atomic Nucleus Ernest Rutherford Gold foil experiment
pH Scale S.P.L. Sørensen Acidity/alkalinity measurement
Oxygen Joseph Priestley / Lavoisier Lavoisier named it “oxygen”
Hydrogen Henry Cavendish First identified gas
Periodic Law (Modern) Henry Moseley Based on atomic number

Biology & Medicine

Discovery / Invention Discoverer / Inventor Remarks
Blood Circulation William Harvey Explained heart and blood flow
Smallpox Vaccine Edward Jenner First vaccine (1796)
Germ Theory of Disease Louis Pasteur Microbes cause disease
Penicillin (First Antibiotic) Alexander Fleming Discovered accidentally in 1928
DNA Double Helix Structure Watson & Crick Proposed in 1953
Microscope Zacharias Janssen (compound) Magnifies microorganisms
Cell (discovery) Robert Hooke Observed cork cells
Mitochondria (discovery) Richard Altmann “Powerhouse of the cell”
Insulin (for diabetes) Frederick Banting & Charles Best Treats high blood sugar
Vaccination (concept) Edward Jenner Introduced smallpox immunization

Computers & Communication

Invention Inventor Remarks
Computer (concept) Charles Babbage “Father of the Computer”
World Wide Web (WWW) Tim Berners-Lee Invented in 1989
Internet Vinton Cerf & Robert Kahn Protocol designers (TCP/IP)
Telephone Alexander Graham Bell Invented in 1876
Radio Guglielmo Marconi Nobel in Physics
Television John Logie Baird Demonstrated first working TV
Mobile Phone Martin Cooper Made first mobile call (1973)
Email Ray Tomlinson Sent first email
Mouse (computer) Douglas Engelbart Introduced in 1960s

Space & Astronomy

Discovery / Invention Person Remarks
Heliocentric Theory Nicolaus Copernicus Sun is at the center of solar system
Telescope (practical) Galileo Galilei First used for astronomical purposes
Laws of Planetary Motion Johannes Kepler Elliptical planetary orbits
Law of Universal Gravitation Isaac Newton Explains planetary motion
Big Bang Theory Georges Lemaître Universe origin theory
Expansion of Universe Edwin Hubble Observed galaxies moving apart
First Satellite (Sputnik 1) Soviet Union Launched in 1957
First Human in Space Yuri Gagarin 1961, Vostok 1
Moon Landing Neil Armstrong 1969, Apollo 11 mission
