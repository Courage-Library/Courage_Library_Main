One Word Substitution for SSC CGL

50 High-Frequency SSC Words

1. What is One Word Substitution?

One Word Substitution means replacing a phrase, group of words, or sentence with a single word.

Example:

Phrase: A person who writes poems

One Word: Poet

SSC often asks 50–60 repeated words every year

3. SSC Exam Tips

Focus on common professional roles, human traits, and scientists

Pay attention to prefixes/suffixes like -ist, -er, -ian

SSC often repeats words like Philanthropist, Bibliophile, Polyglot, Architect, Astronaut

Practice replacing phrases quickly → improves speed in One Word Substitution section
