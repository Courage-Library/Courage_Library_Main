Synonyms & Antonyms for SSC CGL

Complete Vocabulary Guide

1. What are Synonyms and Antonyms?

Synonyms

Words with similar meaning

Example: Happy → Joyful, Cheerful

Antonyms

Words with opposite meaning

Example: Happy → Sad, Miserable

Tip: SSC often asks to choose the closest synonym or antonym for a word in a sentence

2. High-Frequency Synonyms for SSC

Word Synonyms Example in Sentence
Abundant Plentiful, Copious The forest is abundant with trees
Accurate Correct, Exact His answer was accurate
Ambiguous Vague, Unclear The statement was ambiguous
Benevolent Kind, Charitable The benevolent king helped his subjects
Cautious Careful, Wary Be cautious while crossing the road
Diligent Hardworking, Industrious She is a diligent student
Eager Enthusiastic, Keen He is eager to learn
Fragile Delicate, Breakable The vase is fragile
Generous Liberal, Magnanimous He is generous with his wealth
Hostile Unfriendly, Aggressive The dogs were hostile towards strangers

3. High-Frequency Antonyms for SSC

Word Antonyms Example in Sentence
Abundant Scarce, Rare Water is scarce in deserts
Accurate Wrong, Inexact The report was inaccurate
Benevolent Cruel, Malevolent A cruel ruler oppressed his people
Diligent Lazy, Negligent A lazy student never succeeds
Eager Reluctant, Unwilling She was reluctant to speak
Fragile Strong, Sturdy The bridge is sturdy
Generous Stingy, Miserly He is stingy with money
Hostile Friendly, Amiable The neighbors are friendly
Ambiguous Clear, Obvious His instructions were clear
Cautious Careless, Rash Do not be rash in driving

4. SSC Exam Tips

Read the full sentence carefully → meaning may change in context

Synonyms are often tested with words like Abundant, Diligent, Benevolent, Eager, Hostile

Antonyms are tested with words like Scarce, Lazy, Stingy, Cruel, Careless

Watch out for tricky options → SSC often gives words that are partially similar but not exact synonyms

Memorize 200–300 high-frequency words → most questions repeat from previous years

5. SSC CGL Previous Year Questions

Q1. Select the synonym of Diligent

Options: (a) Lazy (b) Hardworking (c) Careless (d) Indifferent

Answer: (b) Hardworking

Q2. Select the antonym of Hostile

Options: (a) Friendly (b) Aggressive (c) Unkind (d) Cruel

Answer: (a) Friendly

Q3. Select the synonym of Ambiguous

Options: (a) Clear (b) Vague (c) Exact (d) Definite

Answer: (b) Vague

Q4. Select the antonym of Generous

Options: (a) Kind (b) Liberal (c) Stingy (d) Charitable

Answer: (c) Stingy

Q5. Select the synonym of Eager

Options: (a) Keen (b) Reluctant (c) Lazy (d) Indifferent

Answer: (a) Keen
