Mixed Practice Sets with Explanations for SSC CGL

Complete Practice & Strategy Guide

1. Purpose of Mixed Practice Sets

SSC often mixes multiple topics in one set

Improve speed + accuracy

Identify your weak areas

Simulate real exam conditions

Topics included:

  • Spot the Error (Noun, Verb, Pronoun, Article, Preposition, Adjective/Adverb)
  • Sentence Improvement & Correction
  • Cloze Test (Grammar & Vocabulary)
  • Sentence Completion
  • Reading Comprehension

2. Mixed Practice Set – Part A (Grammar & Vocabulary)

Q1. Spot the Error:

Every students must submit their report on time

A) Every students

B) must submit

C) their

D) on time

Q2. Sentence Improvement:

She don't has any idea about the meeting

A) She doesn't has any idea about the meeting

B) She don't have any idea about the meeting

C) She doesn't have any idea about the meeting

D) She don't has no idea about the meeting

Q3. Cloze Test (Grammar):

Yesterday, I ___ (1) ___ to the market and ___ (2) ___ some fruits

1: go / went / goes

2: buy / bought / buying

Q4. Cloze Test (Vocabulary):

He was very ___ (1) ___ after winning the match

1: happy / elated / sad

Q5. Sentence Completion:

She decided to ___ the challenge bravely

face / accept / ignore

3. Mixed Practice Set – Part B (Reading & Logic)

Q6. RC Passage:

Deforestation is a major threat to the environment. It reduces biodiversity, affects the climate, and causes soil erosion. Conservation efforts are needed to protect forests and wildlife.

1. What is the main problem discussed?

2. Name one effect of deforestation

3. Why is conservation important?

4. What does "biodiversity" refer to?

Q7. Sentence Correction:

He was very happy, he won the lottery

1. He was very happy, he won the lottery

2. He was very happy because he won the lottery

3. He was very happy, because he won the lottery

4. He was very happy but he won the lottery

Q8. Spot the Error:

The informations given by the teacher is correct

A) The

B) informations

C) given by the teacher

D) is correct

Q9. Sentence Completion:

The manager was pleased with the team's ___

performance / effort / result

Q10. Cloze Test (Mixed):

He decided to ___ (1) ___ the problem immediately. It was a ___ (2) ___ situation

1: solve / solved / solving

2: critical / minor / trivial

4. Hidden Answers for Self-Practice

Q5: face

  • 2. Reduces biodiversity / affects climate / causes soil erosion
  • 3. To protect forests and wildlife
  • 4. Variety of species

Q7: 2) He was very happy because he won the lottery

5. SSC Exam Tips

Time yourself → 1 set ≈ 10–12 minutes

Read instructions carefully

Attempt easier questions first, then difficult ones

Review your mistakes → focus on weak topics

Practice mixed sets regularly → increases speed and accuracy
