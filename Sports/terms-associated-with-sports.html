Terms Associated with Sports

Reference: Lucent GK, NCERT Class 6–12

Cricket

Term Meaning
LBW "Leg Before Wicket" – batsman is out if the ball hits the pad and would have hit the stumps.
Yorker A delivery that pitches right at the batsman’s feet or base of the stumps.
Googly A deceptive delivery bowled by a leg-spinner that turns in the opposite direction.
Duck When a batsman is out without scoring any run.
Silly Point A close fielding position near the batsman on the off side.

SSC Tip: Googly and Yorker are commonly asked.

Football

Term Meaning
Offside A player is offside if he/she is nearer to the opponent's goal than both the ball and the second-last defender when the ball is played to them.
Hat-trick When a player scores 3 goals in a single match.
Free Kick Awarded after a foul; the ball is kicked without interference.
Corner Kick When the ball crosses the goal line, last touched by a defender (no goal scored).
Yellow Card A caution to a player for misconduct. Two yellow cards = 1 red card.

SSC Tip: “Offside” and “Hat-trick” appear regularly in static GK questions.

Tennis

Term Meaning
Deuce When the score is 40–40. The player must win two consecutive points to win the game.
Ace A serve that is untouched by the opponent.
Tiebreak A special game to decide a set tied at 6–6.
Rally Exchange of shots between players before a point is won.
Break Point When the receiving player is one point away from winning the game.

SSC Tip: Ace and Deuce are often asked as direct MCQs.

Hockey

Term Meaning
Penalty Corner Awarded for defensive foul; attacking team takes a shot from the backline.
Push A simple stroke to pass the ball without lifting it.
Scoop A raised hit where the ball is lifted off the ground.
Dribble Controlling the ball with the stick while moving.

SSC Tip: "Penalty Corner" is important for match-the-pair questions

Badminton

Term Meaning
Smash A powerful downward shot aimed to finish the rally.
Drop Shot A gentle shot that barely crosses the net, landing close to it.
Clear A high shot sent deep into the opponent’s court.
Rally Point System where each rally results in a point.

SSC Tip: Smash is the most frequently mentioned term.

Wrestling & Boxing

Term Meaning
Knockout (KO) When an opponent is rendered unable to continue the fight.
Bout A match between two fighters.
Takedown Bringing the opponent to the mat and controlling them.
Technical Fall Winning a match by leading a large margin (wrestling).

SSC Tip: "Knockout" is common in general awareness sets.

Athletics

Term Meaning
Relay A team event where each runner completes a segment and passes a baton.
Steeplechase A distance race with barriers and water jumps (usually 3000m).
Heptathlon Women’s combined event of 7 track & field disciplines.
Decathlon Men's combined event of 10 track & field disciplines.

SSC Tip: Relay and Steeplechase are often asked in basic sports GK
