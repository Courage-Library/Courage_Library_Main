International Sports Organizations

Key governing bodies in world sports

1. IOC -- International Olympic Committee

  • Full Form: International Olympic Committee
  • Headquarters: Lausanne, Switzerland
  • Founded: 1894 by Pierre de Coubertin
  • Role:
    • Governs the Olympic Movement
    • Organizes Summer & Winter Olympics
    • Maintains the Olympic Charter and symbols (rings, flame, motto)
  • Fun Fact: Motto -- "Citius, Altius, Fortius" (Faster, Higher, Stronger)

SSC Tip: IOC = Olympics = Lausanne

2. FIFA -- Fédération Internationale de Football Association

  • Full Form: International Federation of Association Football (French acronym: FIFA)
  • Headquarters: Zürich, Switzerland
  • Founded: 1904
  • Role:
    • Governs international football
    • Organizes FIFA World Cup (Men's and Women's)
    • Sets rules and rankings for national teams
  • Current Member Nations: 211

SSC Tip: FIFA = Football = World Cup = Zürich

3. ICC -- International Cricket Council

  • Full Form: International Cricket Council
  • Headquarters: Dubai, United Arab Emirates
  • Founded: 1909 (as Imperial Cricket Conference)
  • Role:
    • Governs international cricket (ODI, T20I, Test)
    • Organizes Men's and Women's World Cups, T20 World Cups
    • Publishes player rankings
  • ICC Tournaments: World Cup, T20 World Cup, Champions Trophy, WTC

SSC Tip: ICC = Cricket = Dubai

4. BWF -- Badminton World Federation

  • Full Form: Badminton World Federation
  • Headquarters: Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia
  • Founded: 1934 (as IBF)
  • Role:
    • Governs international badminton
    • Organizes Thomas & Uber Cups, BWF World Championships
    • Maintains global rankings
  • Current Popular Indian Players: PV Sindhu, Lakshya Sen

SSC Tip: BWF = Badminton = Malaysia

5. FIDE -- Fédération Internationale des Échecs

  • Full Form: International Chess Federation
  • Headquarters: Lausanne, Switzerland
  • Founded: 1924
  • Role:
    • Governs international chess
    • Organizes Chess Olympiads, World Championships
    • Issues Elo ratings for players

SSC Tip: FIDE = Chess = Elo Ratings

6. WADA -- World Anti-Doping Agency

  • Full Form: World Anti-Doping Agency
  • Headquarters: Montreal, Canada
  • Founded: 1999
  • Role:
    • Promotes fair play in sports by fighting doping
    • Maintains World Anti-Doping Code
    • Publishes list of banned substances and conducts athlete testing

SSC Tip: WADA = Anti-Doping = Montreal

7. IPC -- International Paralympic Committee

  • Full Form: International Paralympic Committee
  • Headquarters: Bonn, Germany
  • Founded: 1989
  • Role:
    • Governs Paralympic Games
    • Promotes sports for athletes with disabilities
    • Organizes Summer & Winter Paralympics
  • Note: Paralympics occur shortly after the Olympics in the same host city.

SSC Tip: IPC = Disabled Sports = Bonn
