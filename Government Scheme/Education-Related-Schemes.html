Education-Related-Schemes

Reference: Lucent GK, NCERT Class 6–12

1. Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan

Feature Details
Objective Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education from preschool to Class 12
Scope Covers entire school education system
Merged Schemes SSA + RMSA + Teacher Education
Focus Areas Access, equity, quality, digital initiatives, and learning outcomes
Key Interventions ICT in schools, gender inclusion, vocational education, infrastructure support
Launch Year 2018 (merged framework)
Ministry Ministry of Education (Department of School Education & Literacy)

Key Point: Covers entire school education system under one umbrella scheme (pre-school to Class 12)

2. Mid-Day Meal Scheme (Now PM POSHAN)

Feature Details
Objective Improve nutritional status and increase school attendance
Target Group Children of classes I–VIII in government and government-aided schools
Key Benefits Hot cooked meals on working days
Impact Enhanced enrollment, retention, and attendance, especially among poor children
Renamed As PM POSHAN (Poshan Shakti Nirman) in 2021
Ministry Ministry of Education
Additional Support Nutrition gardens and health check-ups in schools

Exam Tip: Remember the renaming to PM POSHAN in 2021 and its expanded nutritional components

3. SWAYAM (Study Webs of Active Learning for Young Aspiring Minds)

Feature Details
Objective Provide free, quality online education for all
Type MOOC (Massive Open Online Courses) platform
Target Audience School and college students, teachers, and lifelong learners
Courses Offered UG, PG, school-level, NPTEL (engineering), law, teacher training
Key Features Free content, with certification on payment after proctored exam
Launch Year 2017
Coordinators NPTEL (IITs), UGC, IGNOU, NCERT
Ministry Ministry of Education

Note: India's flagship digital education initiative aligned with NEP 2020 goals

4. National Digital Education Architecture (NDEAR)

Feature Details
Objective Create a unified digital ecosystem for school education
Focus Areas Interoperable digital infrastructure, student tracking, teacher support
Part of National Education Policy (NEP) 2020 implementation
Supports School governance, curriculum design, learning resources, analytics
Vision “Open Digital Ecosystem” for education
Ministry Ministry of Education
Platforms Linked DIKSHA, UDISE+, NAS, Academic Bank of Credits

Key Concept: Creates "Open Digital Ecosystem" for education governance and delivery

Scheme Comparison for Exams

  • Samagra Shiksha → School education (pre-school to 12)
  • PM POSHAN → Nutrition + Education integration
  • SWAYAM → Digital courses with certification
  • NDEAR → Digital infrastructure backbone for NEP 2020
