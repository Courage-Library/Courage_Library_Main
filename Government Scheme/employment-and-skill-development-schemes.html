Employment & Skill Development Schemes

Government initiatives for employment generation and skill enhancement

1. MGNREGA (Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act)

Feature Details
Objective Provide livelihood security through guaranteed rural employment
Guarantee 100 days of wage employment per household annually
Coverage All rural districts of India
Type Demand-driven & rights-based program
Wage Payment Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) into bank/post office accounts
Additional Benefits Focus on women participation, rural asset creation
Launch Year 2006
Ministry Ministry of Rural Development

SSC Tip: MGNREGA = Rural employment guarantee = 100 days/year = Ministry of Rural Development

2. PM Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY)

Feature Details
Objective Enable youth to take industry-relevant skill training
Benefit Free short-term training programs across sectors
Certification NSDC-approved certification after assessment
Job Linkage Focus on placement assistance post-training
Target Group Unemployed youth (school/college dropouts, etc.)
Key Bodies Involved National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC)
Launch Year 2015
Ministry Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship

SSC Tip: PMKVY = Skill development = NSDC certification = Ministry of Skill Development

3. Start-Up India / Stand-Up India

Scheme Start-Up India Stand-Up India
Objective Promote innovation & entrepreneurship Provide loans to SC/ST & women entrepreneurs
Benefits Tax exemption, easier compliance, funding support Bank loans from ₹10 lakh to ₹1 crore
Target Group All Indian entrepreneurs (Start-Up India) SC/ST and Women (Stand-Up India)
Launch Year 2016 2016
Ministries DPIIT (Start-Up India) DFS -- Finance Ministry (Stand-Up India)

SSC Tip:

  • Start-Up India = For all entrepreneurs = DPIIT
  • Stand-Up India = For SC/ST & women = Finance Ministry

4. National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme (NAPS)

Feature Details
Objective Promote apprenticeship training among youth and industries
Benefits Financial incentives to establishments for hiring apprentices
Key Incentives Partial stipend reimbursement by government
Focus Areas On-the-job training + basic theoretical instruction
Eligible Beneficiaries School/college dropouts, ITI pass-outs, fresher graduates
Launch Year 2016
Ministry Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship

SSC Tip: NAPS = Apprenticeship training = Stipend reimbursement = Same ministry as PMKVY
