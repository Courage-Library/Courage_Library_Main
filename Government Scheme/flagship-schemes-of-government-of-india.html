Flagship Schemes of the Government of India

Reference: Lucent GK, NCERT Class 6–12

PM-KISAN (Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi)

Feature Details
Objective Provide income support to small & marginal farmers
Benefit ₹6,000/year in 3 equal installments of ₹2,000
Eligibility Farmers owning up to 2 hectares of land
Mode of Transfer Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) to bank accounts
Launch Year 2019
Ministry Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

PM Awas Yojana (Urban & Rural)

Feature Details
Objective “Housing for All” by 2022
Components PMAY-Urban & PMAY-Gramin
Benefit Subsidy on housing loans under Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme (CLSS)
Target Group EWS, LIG, MIG, rural poor
Implementation Urban: MoHUA (Housing & Urban Affairs) Rural: MoRD (Rural Development)

PM Ujjwala Yojana

Feature Details
Objective Provide clean cooking fuel (LPG) to BPL women
Benefit Free LPG connection, first refill, and stove
Target Group Women from BPL households
Ministry Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas
Launch Year 2016

PM Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Feature Details
Objective Provide insurance coverage and financial support to farmers
Coverage Pre- and post-harvest losses due to natural calamities
Premium (Paid by Farmer) 2% for Kharif, 1.5% for Rabi, 5% for Commercial crops
Launch Year 2016
Ministry Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

PM Jan Dhan Yojana

Feature Details
Objective Universal access to banking facilities
Benefit Zero-balance bank account, RuPay debit card, insurance
Insurance Cover ₹1 lakh accidental + ₹30,000 life insurance (conditions apply)
Focus Financial inclusion, DBT, and digital payments
Launch Year 2014
Ministry Ministry of Finance

Ayushman Bharat – PM Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY)

Feature Details
Objective Provide health coverage to economically weaker sections
Coverage ₹5 lakh/family/year for secondary & tertiary treatment
Target Beneficiaries 10+ crore families (approx. 50 crore individuals)
Type World's largest publicly funded health assurance scheme
Mode Cashless & paperless via empaneled hospitals
Launch Year 2018
Ministry Ministry of Health and Family Welfare

Atmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyan

Feature Details
Objective Economic revival post COVID-19
Total Financial Package Approx. ₹20 lakh crore
Focus Areas MSMEs, agriculture, infrastructure, health, education
Initiatives under it Emergency Credit Line Guarantee Scheme, PM SVANidhi, Production Linked Incentive (PLI) schemes
Launch Year 2020
Coordinating Ministries Multiple (Finance, Commerce, MSME, etc.)
