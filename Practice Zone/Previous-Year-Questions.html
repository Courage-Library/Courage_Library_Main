SSC-CGL Exam

1. Number System

SSC CGL 2022

The LCM of two numbers is 180 and their HCF is 12. If one number is 36, find the other.

View Answer

Other number = (LCM × HCF)/given number = (180×12)/36

60

SSC CGL 2021

Unit digit of 3¹²⁵ + 7¹²⁵?

View Answer

3 powers: 3,9,7,1 → 125 mod 4 =1 → 3¹²⁵=3

7 powers: 7,9,3,1 → 7¹²⁵=7 → Sum=3+7=10

Unit digit = 0

2. Percentage

SSC CGL 2020

A trader marks price 20% above CP. If he allows 10% discount, find profit %.

View Answer

CP=100 → MP=120 → SP=120×0.9=108 → Profit=8

8%

SSC CGL 2019

Population increased from 1,00,000 to 1,20,000 → Find % increase.

View Answer

(120000−100000)/100000×100

20%

3. Profit, Loss & Discount

SSC CGL 2022

A sold an article at 20% profit. If he had sold it for ₹200 more, profit would be 40%. Find CP.

View Answer

1000

SSC CGL 2021

A trader buys 10 kg sugar @ ₹40/kg, sells 8 kg @ ₹50/kg → Gain/Loss?

View Answer

CP=400, SP=8×50=400

No profit/loss

4. Simple & Compound Interest

SSC CGL 2020

SI on ₹5000 for 2 years at 8% p.a.?

View Answer

SI=(5000×8×2)/100

800

SSC CGL 2019

CI: ₹2000 at 10% p.a. for 2 years?

View Answer

Year1: 2000×0.1=200 → 2200

Year2: 2200×0.1=220 → Total=2420

2420

6. Average

SSC CGL 2020

Average of 5 numbers 12,15,18,20,x=16 → Find x

View Answer

Sum=5×16=80 → 12+15+18+20=65 → x=80−65

15

SSC CGL 2019

Weighted average: 3 students scored 80, 4 students scored 90 → Find average

View Answer

(3×80 + 4×90)/7 = (240+360)/7=600/7

≈85.71

7. Time, Speed & Distance

Two trains 120 km apart → Speeds 40 km/h & 60 km/h → Meet in?
