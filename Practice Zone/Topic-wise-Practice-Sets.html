SSC-CGL Exam

1. Number System

Q1. LCM of 12, 15, 20?

View Answer

LCM = 2² × 3 × 5 = 60

Q2. HCF of 48, 180?

View Answer

HCF = 12

Q3. Unit digit of 7³⁵?

View Answer

7 powers cycle: 7,9,3,1 → 35 mod 4 = 3

Unit digit = 3

Q4. Simplify: √50 × √2

View Answer

√100 = 10

2. Percentage

Q1. 20% of 250?

View Answer

50

Q2. Successive discounts: 20%, 10% → Effective discount?

View Answer

1−(0.8×0.9)=0.28

28%

3. Profit, Loss & Discount

Q1. CP = ₹500, SP = ₹600 → Profit?

View Answer

Profit = 600−500 = 100

Q2. Marked price = ₹1000, discount 10% → SP?

View Answer

SP = 1000×0.9

900

4. Simple & Compound Interest

Q1. SI on ₹5000 at 5% p.a. for 2 years?

View Answer

SI = (5000×5×2)/100

500

Q2. CI annually: ₹1000 at 10% for 2 years?

View Answer

Year1: 1000×0.1=100 → 1100

Year2: 1100×0.1=110 → Total = 1210

1210

5. Ratio & Proportion

Q1. Divide ₹600 in 2:3 ratio?

View Answer

2+3=5 → 600×2/5=240, 600×3/5=360

240 and 360

Q2. Partnership: A: ₹5000, B: ₹7000 → Profit ₹1200 → Share of A?

View Answer

5000:7000=5:7 → A's share = 1200×5/12

500

6. Average

Q1. Average of 10, 20, 30, 40?

View Answer

(10+20+30+40)/4

25

Q2. Weighted average: 5×2 + 3×3?

View Answer

(5×2 + 3×3)/(5+3) = (10+9)/8

2.375

7. Time, Speed & Distance

Q1. Car travels 120 km in 2 hr → Speed?

View Answer

120/2

60 km/h

Q2. Two trains 100 km apart, moving towards each other 40 km/h & 60 km/h → Meet in?

View Answer

Time = 100/(40+60)

1 hour

8. Time & Work

Q1. A can do a work in 10 days, B in 15 days → Together?

View Answer

1/10 +1/15=1/6

6 days

Q2. Pipe fills tank in 12 hr, emptying pipe 16 hr → Net time?

View Answer

1/12−1/16=1/48

48 hours

(x−3)

Q2. Solve: x²−5x+6=0

View Answer

x=2,3

View Answer

View Answer
