Stadiums and Sports Venues (India Focused)

Key sporting arenas across India

1. Cricket Stadiums

Stadium Name Location Special Notes
Narendra Modi Stadium Ahmedabad, Gujarat Largest cricket stadium in the world (Capacity: ~1.3 lakh); Venue for major IPL and international finals
Wankhede Stadium Mumbai, Maharashtra Hosted 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup Final; Home ground of Mumbai Indians
Eden Gardens Kolkata, West Bengal One of the oldest and most iconic cricket stadiums in India; Home of Kolkata Knight Riders
M.A. Chidambaram Stadium Chennai, Tamil Nadu Also known as Chepauk Stadium; One of the oldest stadiums in India; Home of Chennai Super Kings
Arun Jaitley Stadium Delhi Formerly Feroz Shah Kotla; Historic venue; Home of Delhi Capitals
HPCA Stadium Dharamshala, Himachal Pradesh Known for its scenic location in the Himalayas; Hosts IPL and international matches

SSC Tip: Narendra Modi Stadium = Largest in the world = Ahmedabad.

2. Hockey Stadiums

Stadium Name Location Special Notes
Kalinga Stadium Bhubaneswar, Odisha Hosted Men's Hockey World Cup 2018 & 2023
Major Dhyan Chand National Stadium New Delhi Used in 2010 Commonwealth Games; Named after the hockey legend
Birsa Munda Hockey Stadium Rourkela, Odisha One of the newest and largest hockey stadiums in India

SSC Tip: Kalinga = Bhubaneswar = Hockey World Cup venue.

3. Football Venues

Stadium Name Location Special Notes
Salt Lake Stadium (Vivekananda Yuba Bharati Krirangan) Kolkata, West Bengal One of the largest football stadiums in India; Hosted FIFA U-17 WC Final 2017
Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium Kochi, Kerala FIFA U-17 World Cup 2017 venue; Also used for ISL matches
Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium New Delhi Multipurpose stadium; Hosted athletics & football events in Commonwealth Games 2010
Fatorda Stadium Margao, Goa Prominent ISL venue; Home ground of FC Goa
Kanteerava Stadium Bengaluru, Karnataka Home ground for Bengaluru FC

SSC Tip: Salt Lake Stadium = Kolkata = Football

4. Multipurpose Stadiums & Events

Stadium Name Location Used For
Indira Gandhi Indoor Stadium New Delhi Gymnastics, wrestling, kabaddi, Commonwealth Games 2010
Jawaharlal Nehru Stadium (Delhi) New Delhi Athletics, Football, Ceremonies of CWG 2010
Shiv Chhatrapati Sports Complex Pune, Maharashtra Athletics, badminton, shooting
Gachibowli Stadium Hyderabad, Telangana Athletics, Kabaddi, Badminton

SSC Tip: Indira Gandhi Stadium = Indoor Events = Delhi

Summary Table (Sport-wise)

Sport Stadiums / Venues
Cricket Narendra Modi (Ahmedabad), Wankhede (Mumbai), Eden Gardens (Kolkata)
Hockey Kalinga (Bhubaneswar), Dhyan Chand (Delhi), Birsa Munda (Rourkela)
Football Salt Lake (Kolkata), Nehru (Kochi/Delhi), Fatorda (Goa)
Multipurpose Indira Gandhi Indoor (Delhi), Shiv Chhatrapati (Pune)

Exam Strategy

  • • Focus on "Location vs Sport" format.
  • • Salt Lake, Kalinga, Narendra Modi Stadiums are frequently asked.
  • • CWG 2010 Delhi venues (Indira Gandhi & Nehru Stadium) often appear in older sets.
