Cloze Test for SSC CGL

Grammar & Vocabulary Strategy

1. What is a Cloze Test?

Cloze Test is a passage with blanks, where you are required to fill the blanks with the most appropriate words.

SSC Cloze Test tests:

  • Grammar → tenses, prepositions, conjunctions, articles
  • Vocabulary → synonyms, antonyms, collocations, phrasal verbs
  • Comprehension → context-based choice

Example:

She ___ (go) to the market every morning.

Answer: goes → subject-verb agreement

2. Strategy for Grammar-Based Cloze Test

1. Check Subject-Verb Agreement

  • Singular → verb ends with -s, plural → base form
  • Example: The teacher ___ (teach) English. → teaches

2. Identify Tense

  • Past, present, future based on context
  • Example: Yesterday he ___ (buy) a book. → bought

3. Look for Prepositions

  • Common SSC-tested: in, on, at, for, by, of, with
  • Example: He is good ___ mathematics. → at

4. Articles

  • Use a, an, the correctly
  • Example: I saw ___ elephant in the zoo. → an

5. Conjunctions

  • Connect ideas using: and, but, or, although, because, since

6. Pronouns & Adjectives

  • Ensure correct reference and degree
  • Example: This is the best book I have ever read

3. Strategy for Vocabulary-Based Cloze Test

1. Synonyms & Antonyms

  • Use words that fit the meaning of the sentence
  • Example: She was very ___ (happy) after the exam. → elated / joyful

2. Collocations

  • Words that usually occur together
  • Example: Make a decision, Take a risk, Heavy rain

3. Context Clues

  • Look at other words in the sentence to infer meaning
  • Example: He is very gregarious, always talking and laughing with everyone. → sociable

4. Idioms & Phrases

  • Sometimes SSC tests idiomatic usage
  • Example: He decided to bite the bullet. → face the difficulty bravely

4. SSC Cloze Test Common Patterns

Pattern Strategy Example
Verb tense Check time context She ___ (go) to school yesterday. → went
Prepositions Look at nouns/verbs nearby He is good ___ cricket. → at
Articles Identify countable/uncountable I saw ___ eagle in the sky. → an
Synonyms Meaning matches context The test was easy → simple
Antonyms Sentence indicates contrast He is poor, but his brother is ___ → rich
Idioms & phrases Fit context He was over the moon → very happy

5. Sample Cloze Test

Grammar-Based Cloze Test

Last week, I ___ (1) ___ to the market to buy some fruits. I ___ (2) ___ an apple and an orange. The shopkeeper ___ (3) ___ me about the prices. I ___ (4) ___ that the apple was very expensive. However, I ___ (5) ___ to buy it because it was fresh.

Blanks:

1. go / went / goes

2. buy / bought / buying

3. told / tell / telling

4. realize / realized / realizing

5. decide / decided / deciding

Click for Answers

1. went

2. bought

3. told

4. realized

5. decided

Vocabulary-Based Cloze Test

The manager was very ___ (1) ___ with the team's performance. Everyone worked ___ (2) ___ and the results were ___ (3) ___. The meeting ended on a ___ (4) ___ note.

Options:

1. (a) pleased (b) angry (c) sad

2. (a) diligently (b) lazily (c) slowly

3. (a) disappointing (b) excellent (c) poor

4. (a) positive (b) negative (c) neutral

Click for Answers

1. pleased

2. diligently

3. excellent

4. positive

6. SSC Exam Tips for Cloze Test

Read the entire sentence before filling the blank

For grammar blanks, check subject, verb, tense, prepositions

For vocabulary blanks, consider synonyms, idioms, collocations

Eliminate obviously wrong options first → saves time

Practice previous 10 years' SSC Cloze Tests → patterns repeat
