Tenses for SSC CGL

Complete Grammar Guide

1. What are Tenses?

Tenses show the time of action in a sentence.

Present

happening now / habitual

Past

happened before

Future

will happen later

Present → She reads a book

Past → She read a book yesterday

Future → She will read a book tomorrow

2. Basic Tense Patterns

Tense Structure Example SSC Focus
Present Simple Subject + V1(s/es) He plays cricket Habitual actions, error spotting
Present Continuous Subject + is/are + V-ing He is playing cricket Ongoing actions, missing/extra "is/are"
Present Perfect Subject + has/have + V3 He has played cricket Misuse of has/have, past time indicators
Past Simple Subject + V2 He played cricket Regular/irregular verbs, wrong verb form
Past Continuous Subject + was/were + V-ing He was playing cricket Subject-verb agreement, time mismatch
Past Perfect Subject + had + V3 He had played cricket before he left Confusion with past simple
Future Simple Subject + will + V1 He will play cricket Will/Shall errors, do/does confusion
Future Continuous Subject + will be + V-ing He will be playing cricket Wrong use of will be/shall be
Future Perfect Subject + will have + V3 He will have played cricket by 5 PM Error spotting with by/within time markers

3. Common SSC Error-Spotting Patterns

Pattern 1: Wrong Verb Form

She goes yesterday to school

She went yesterday to school

Pattern 2: Confusion Between Present Perfect & Past Simple

I have seen him yesterday

I saw him yesterday

Tip: Use Past Simple with specific past time (yesterday, last year)

Pattern 3: Wrong Use of Continuous Form

He is going to school yesterday

He was going to school yesterday

Pattern 4: Subject-Verb Agreement in Tense

The students was studying for exams

The students were studying for exams

Pattern 5: Future Tense Traps

  • Will/shall misuse → "I will go", not "I shall going"
  • Use of future continuous → correct form: will be + V-ing

He will be playing cricket at 5 PM

Pattern 6: Present Perfect Continuous vs Present Continuous

I am living here for five years

I have been living here for five years

4. SSC CGL Previous Year Questions

Q1. She ___ to the market yesterday.

Options: (a) goes (b) went (c) has gone (d) will go

Answer: (b) went

Q2. I ___ him for ten years.

Options: (a) know (b) knew (c) have known (d) was knowing

Answer: (c) have known

Q3. They ___ playing cricket at 6 PM tomorrow.

Options: (a) will (b) will be (c) will have (d) shall

Answer: (b) will be

Q4. He ___ his homework before he went out.

Options: (a) completes (b) completed (c) had completed (d) has completed

Answer: (c) had completed

Q5. The students ___ studying since morning.

Options: (a) is (b) was (c) have been (d) have been

Answer: (c) have been

5. Quick Recap

  • Identify time markers → yesterday → past simple, since/for → present perfect, by/within → future perfect
  • Check subject-verb agreement in all tenses
  • Watch for continuous vs simple forms
  • SSC often tests present perfect vs past simple and future continuous/future perfect
