Industries in India – Iron, Steel, Cotton, IT, etc.

Delhi Police Exams – Economic & Human Geography

Introduction: The Industrial Backbone of India

Industry plays a vital role in transforming raw materials into finished goods, providing employment, and boosting economic development.

India has a diverse industrial base — from traditional textile units to high-tech IT hubs.

  • Primary Industry: Uses natural resources (e.g., mining).
  • Secondary Industry: Manufacturing goods (e.g., steel).
  • Tertiary Industry: Services (e.g., IT, banking).
Memory Trick: "Primary digs, Secondary builds, Tertiary serves."

Exam Tricks & Mnemonics

"I Just Cook Awesome Information"

→ Iron, Jute, Cotton, Automobile, IT

"MACHO" for Iron & Steel Plants:

  • M – Manganese (needed for alloy)
  • A – Abundant coal
  • C – Cheap labor
  • H – Heavy water supply
  • O – Ore (iron) availability

Cotton → West, Jute → East, IT → South.
