Advent of Europeans & British East India Company

Delhi Police Exam History Section

Why Europeans came to India?

Main reason = Trade

India at that time was famous for spices, cotton, silk, indigo, muslin, perfumes, precious stones etc.

Which Europeans came first?

Europeans Arrival in India Important Notes
Portuguese First (1498) Vasco da Gama arrived at Calicut. Established first factory at Cochin.
Dutch (Netherlands) Second (1605) Strong in spice islands (Indonesia)
British Third (1600) East India Company formed by Royal Charter in 1600
Danish Fourth Small presence in Bengal & Tamil Nadu
French Last (1664) French East India Company formed

Key fact: Portuguese were 1st, French were last.

Portuguese Rule in India

First European to come & stay permanently

Vasco da Gama landed in Calicut (1498)

Goa captured by Afonso de Albuquerque (1510)

Introduced: tobacco, tomatoes, potatoes, chillies to India

(very important exam point)

British East India Company

Established: 1600 (Queen Elizabeth I Charter)

First English factory: Surat (1613)

Later established factories at Madras, Bombay, Calcutta

Important British Battles

Battle Year Result
Battle of Plassey 1757 British defeated Siraj ud-Daulah (foundation of British rule in India)
Battle of Buxar 1764 British defeated Bengal + Awadh + Mughal (gave Diwani rights)

After Buxar → British became real rulers in India.

Why British became dominant over other Europeans?

Strong navy

Superior military organization

Used diplomacy + divide and rule

Strong private capital support

Quick Recap (5 Short Points)

Portuguese came first → Vasco da Gama (1498)

British East India Company formed 1600

English first factory → Surat (1613)

Plassey (1757) → British political power starts

Buxar (1764) → Confirmed British control in Bengal
