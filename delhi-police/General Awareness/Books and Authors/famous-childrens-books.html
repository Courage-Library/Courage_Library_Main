Famous Children’s Books

Delhi Police Exams - GK Section

Introduction to Children’s Literature

Children’s books nurture imagination, creativity, and values. Many have become exam classics. Know the author and main character for every book listed below.

Memory Trick: K–R–D–C → Kipling, Rowling, Dahl, Carroll (“Kids Read Dreamy Classics!”)

Famous Works & Authors

The Jungle Book – Rudyard Kipling

  • Author: Rudyard Kipling
  • Published: 1894
  • Genre: Adventure / Children’s fiction
  • Main Character: Mowgli (with Bagheera, Baloo, Shere Khan)
  • Theme: Friendship, courage, survival
Kipling Keeps Kids in the Jungle!

Harry Potter Series – J.K. Rowling

  • Author: J.K. Rowling
  • Published: 1997–2007 (7 books)
  • Main Character: Harry Potter
  • Theme: Magic, friendship, fight of good vs evil
Rowling’s Magic Rules the World.

Charlie and the Chocolate Factory – Roald Dahl

  • Author: Roald Dahl
  • Published: 1964
  • Main Characters: Charlie Bucket, Willy Wonka
  • Theme: Imagination, honesty, greed vs virtue
Dahl’s Chocolate Factory – Sweet Story, Moral Core.

Alice in Wonderland – Lewis Carroll

  • Author: Lewis Carroll
  • Published: 1865
  • Main Character: Alice
  • Theme: Imagination, curiosity, reality vs dream
Carroll’s Alice Creates a Curious World.

PYQs (Delhi Police, SSC & State Exams)

Who wrote The Jungle Book?

A) R.K. Narayan   B) Rudyard Kipling   C) Roald Dahl   D) Lewis Carroll

Show Answer

B) Rudyard Kipling

Harry Potter Series was written by —

A) Suzanne Collins   B) J.K. Rowling   C) Enid Blyton   D) Roald Dahl

Show Answer

B) J.K. Rowling

The novel Charlie and the Chocolate Factory is authored by —

A) Roald Dahl   B) Rudyard Kipling   C) J.K. Rowling   D) Lewis Carroll

Show Answer

A) Roald Dahl

Who wrote Alice in Wonderland?

A) Charles Dickens   B) Lewis Carroll   C) Mark Twain   D) H.G. Wells

Show Answer

B) Lewis Carroll

The Jungle Book features which character?

A) Peter Pan   B) Charlie Bucket   C) Mowgli   D) Harry Potter

Show Answer

C) Mowgli

Willy Wonka is a character from —

A) Alice in Wonderland   B) The Jungle Book   C) Charlie and the Chocolate Factory   D) The Wizard of Oz

Show Answer

C) Charlie and the Chocolate Factory

Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry appears in —

A) Alice in Wonderland   B) Harry Potter   C) Charlie and the Chocolate Factory   D) The Jungle Book

Show Answer

B) Harry Potter

Which of the following books was written by a British-Indian author?

A) The Jungle Book   B) The Alchemist   C) Harry Potter   D) Animal Farm

Show Answer

A) The Jungle Book (Rudyard Kipling)

Lewis Carroll’s Alice in Wonderland was published in —

A) 1805   B) 1865   C) 1900   D) 1925

Show Answer

B) 1865

The Jungle Book was adapted into which famous Disney movie?

A) Lion King   B) Jungle Book   C) Tarzan   D) Finding Nemo

Show Answer

B) Jungle Book

Final Concept Recap

  • Rudyard Kipling – The Jungle Book: Adventure & moral lessons
  • J.K. Rowling – Harry Potter: Magic, courage, friendship
  • Roald Dahl – Charlie and the Chocolate Factory: Imagination & goodness
  • Lewis Carroll – Alice in Wonderland: Curiosity & creativity
