Recent Publications (2022–2025 Focus)

Delhi Police Exams - GK Section

Introduction to Recent Publications

Recent publications by political leaders, motivational speakers and global personalities are increasingly asked in Delhi Police and SSC exams. Stay up-to-date with trending books and their authors!

Memory Trick: J–G–M–N–O–S → Jaishankar, Gauri, Modi, Nehru-Bose, Obama, Shivani. (Just Great Minds Narrate Outstanding Stories.)

Recent & Trending Books (2022–2025)

The India Way – S. Jaishankar

  • Author: Dr. S. Jaishankar (External Affairs Minister of India)
  • Published: 2020 (relevant for 2024–25 exams)
  • Theme: India’s foreign policy & global strategy
  • Publisher: HarperCollins India
Jaishankar shows India’s Way in the World.

My Life in Design – Gauri Khan

  • Author: Gauri Khan
  • Published: 2023
  • Theme: Interior design, creativity, celebrity lifestyle
  • Publisher: Penguin Random House
Gauri’s Design = Glamour + Detail.

Modi@20: Dreams Meet Delivery – BluKraft Digital Foundation

  • Editor: BluKraft Digital Foundation
  • Published: 2022
  • Theme: Essays on leadership and governance
  • Contributors: Amit Shah, S. Jaishankar, Nirmala Sitharaman, etc.
Modi@20 → 20 Years of Governance Glory.

Nehru & Bose: Parallel Lives – Rudrangshu Mukherjee

  • Author: Rudrangshu Mukherjee
  • Published: 2014
  • Theme: Comparative biography of Nehru & Subhas Bose
  • Publisher: Penguin Books
Nehru & Bose Walk Together in Parallel Lives.

A Promised Land – Barack Obama

  • Author: Barack Obama (Former U.S. President)
  • Published: 2020
  • Genre: Political memoir
  • Publisher: Crown Publishing
Obama’s Promise = Hope & Leadership.

The Power of One Thought – BK Shivani

  • Author: Sister BK Shivani (Brahma Kumaris Spiritual Teacher)
  • Published: 2023
  • Theme: Mindset transformation & positivity
  • Publisher: HarperCollins India
Shivani’s One Thought = Inner Transformation.

PYQs (Delhi Police, SSC & State Exams)

Who is the author of The India Way?

A) S. Jaishankar   B) Nirmala Sitharaman   C) Amit Shah   D) Dr. Manmohan Singh

Show Answer

A) S. Jaishankar

My Life in Design was written by —

A) Twinkle Khanna   B) Gauri Khan   C) BK Shivani   D) Sudha Murthy

Show Answer

B) Gauri Khan

The book Modi@20: Dreams Meet Delivery is —

A) Autobiography of Narendra Modi   B) A collection of essays on Modi’s leadership   C) Written by Amit Shah   D) A fictional work

Show Answer

B) A collection of essays on Modi’s leadership

Nehru & Bose: Parallel Lives is authored by —

A) Ramachandra Guha   B) Rudrangshu Mukherjee   C) Shashi Tharoor   D) Bipan Chandra

Show Answer

B) Rudrangshu Mukherjee

Who wrote A Promised Land?

A) Nelson Mandela   B) Barack Obama   C) Joe Biden   D) Donald Trump

Show Answer

B) Barack Obama

The Power of One Thought is authored by —

A) BK Shivani   B) Deepak Chopra   C) Sadhguru   D) Robin Sharma

Show Answer

A) BK Shivani

Modi@20: Dreams Meet Delivery was published in which year?

A) 2019   B) 2020   C) 2022   D) 2024

Show Answer

C) 2022

A) Agriculture   B) Indian Foreign Policy   C) Education Reform   D) Rural Development

B) Indian Foreign Policy
