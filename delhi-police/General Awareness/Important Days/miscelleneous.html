Miscellaneous Days

General Awareness – Exam Preparation

Introduction: Understanding Important Days

Apart from national and environmental observances, there are special global and national awareness days celebrating the spirit of democracy, peace, media, education, and social welfare.

These topics are frequently asked in General Awareness (GK) sections of Delhi Police, SSC, and UPSC exams.

Memory Trick: "Vote for Democracy, Speak with Freedom, Teach with Peace, Protect Animals, and Respect Farmers."

National Voters' Day

Aspect Details
Date25th January
Organized ByElection Commission of India (ECI)
Started In2011
ObjectiveTo encourage young people to take part in the electoral process
SignificanceCelebrated on the foundation day of the Election Commission (25 Jan 1950)
Theme (2024)"Nothing Like Voting, I Vote for Sure"
"25 Jan – ECI Birthday = Every Vote Counts."

International Day of Democracy

Aspect Details
Date15th September
Organized ByUnited Nations (UN)
Adopted ByUN General Assembly in 2007
ObjectiveTo promote and uphold democratic values and principles worldwide
Theme (2024)"Empowering the Next Generation"
"15 Sept – Power to the People Day."

World Press Freedom Day

Aspect Details
Date3rd May
Organized ByUNESCO
Started In1993
ObjectiveTo defend media freedom, journalists' safety, and access to information
Theme (2024)"A Press for the Planet: Journalism in the Face of the Environmental Crisis"
"3rd May – Freedom of the Pen Day."

World Teachers' Day

Aspect Details
Date5th October
Organized ByUNESCO, UNICEF & ILO
Started In1994
ObjectiveTo honor teachers and promote quality education worldwide
Theme (2024)"The Teachers We Need for the Education We Want"
NoteIndia celebrates Teachers' Day separately on 5th September (Dr. S. Radhakrishnan's birthday)
"5th Oct – The World Says 'Thank You, Teachers!'"

International Day of Peace

Aspect Details
Date21st September
Organized ByUnited Nations (UN)
Established In1981
ObjectiveTo strengthen global peace and end conflicts worldwide
Theme (2024)"Actions for Peace: Our Ambition for the #GlobalGoals"
"21st Sept – Peace Before Democracy (15 Sept)."

World Animal Day

Aspect Details
Date4th October
Organized ByWorld Animal Protection (WAP)
SignificanceHonors St. Francis of Assisi, the patron saint of animals
ObjectiveTo raise awareness for animal rights and welfare
"4th Oct – For Every Paw and Claw!"

National Farmers' Day (Kisan Diwas)

Aspect Details
Date23rd December
Organized ByGovernment of India
SignificanceMarks the birth anniversary of Chaudhary Charan Singh, the 5th Prime Minister of India and champion of farmers' rights
ObjectiveTo recognize the contribution of Indian farmers to the nation's growth
"23 Dec – Farmer's Day, Chaudhary's Way."
