National Sports of Major Countries

Delhi Police Exams - GK Static Section

Introduction

Every country has a sport that reflects its culture, tradition, and national identity. While some are officially declared national sports, others are traditionally popular as the nation’s pride.

Memory Trick: National Sports = Nation’s Identity through Play.

National Sports of Major Countries

Country National Sport Quick Note / Remark
IndiaHockeyNot officially declared, but historically national game (8 Olympic Golds)
USABaseballNational pastime; Major League Baseball globally famous
CanadaIce HockeyWinter sport (with Lacrosse in Summer)
JapanSumo WrestlingAncient martial art; official national sport

BrazilFootball (Soccer)Pelé, Neymar; 5-time FIFA World Cup champ
AustraliaCricketNational passion; 5-time Cricket World Cup winner

Mnemonic: IUCJ CBA – India, USA, Canada, Japan, China, Brazil, Australia. “In USA, Canadians Jump Cheerfully Before Australia.”

PYQs (Delhi Police, SSC & State Exams)

Which is the national sport of India?

A) Cricket   B) Hockey   C) Kabaddi   D) Football

Show Answer

B) Hockey

Baseball is the national sport of —

A) USA   B) Brazil   C) Australia   D) Canada

Show Answer

A) USA

Which of the following countries has Ice Hockey as its national game?

A) Russia   B) Canada   C) Norway   D) Australia

Show Answer

B) Canada

Sumo wrestling is the national sport of —

A) China   B) Japan   C) Thailand   D) South Korea

Show Answer

B) Japan

Table Tennis is the national sport of —

A) Japan   B) China   C) Singapore   D) Korea

Show Answer

B) China

Which is the national sport of Brazil?

A) Volleyball   B) Hockey   C) Football   D) Cricket

Show Answer

C) Football

Cricket is the national sport of —

A) South Africa   B) Australia   C) India   D) England

Show Answer

B) Australia

Which country recognizes “Kabaddi” as its national sport?

A) India   B) Bangladesh   C) Nepal   D) Bhutan

B) Bangladesh

“Charrería” is the national sport of —

A) Spain   B) Mexico   C) Argentina   D) Portugal

Show Answer

The national sport of Sri Lanka is —

A) Cricket   B) Football   C) Volleyball   D) Tennis
