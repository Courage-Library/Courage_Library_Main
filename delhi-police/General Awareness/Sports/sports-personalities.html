Delhi Police & SSC Exams - GK Section

Introduction

Every competitive exam asks about famous sports personalities, their sports, and achievements. Knowing nicknames and records is essential for static GK in Police, SSC, and Railway exams.

PYQs (Delhi Police, SSC & State Exams)

Who is known as the “God of Cricket”?

A) Kapil Dev   B) Sachin Tendulkar   C) M.S. Dhoni   D) Virat Kohli

Show Answer

B) Sachin Tendulkar

“The Flying Sikh” refers to —

A) Neeraj Chopra   B) Milkha Singh   C) P.T. Usha   D) Abhinav Bindra

Show Answer

B) Milkha Singh

Who won India’s first-ever Olympic Gold in Athletics?

A) Milkha Singh   B) P.T. Usha   C) Neeraj Chopra   D) Bajrang Punia

Show Answer

C) Neeraj Chopra

Which sport is associated with P.V. Sindhu?

A) Tennis   B) Badminton   C) Table Tennis   D) Archery

Show Answer

B) Badminton

“Captain Cool” is the nickname of —

A) Virat Kohli   B) M.S. Dhoni   C) Rohit Sharma   D) Kapil Dev

Show Answer

B) M.S. Dhoni

Major Dhyan Chand is associated with —

A) Hockey   B) Football   C) Athletics   D) Cricket

Show Answer

A) Hockey

Who among the following is a famous Indian Boxer?

A) P.V. Sindhu   B) Mary Kom   C) Vinesh Phogat   D) Saina Nehwal

Show Answer

B) Mary Kom

Sania Mirza is associated with which sport?

A) Table Tennis   B) Badminton   C) Tennis   D) Squash

Show Answer

C) Tennis

Sunil Chhetri plays which sport?

A) Cricket   B) Football   C) Hockey   D) Tennis

Show Answer

B) Football

P.T. Usha is popularly known as —

A) The Sprint Queen   B) Hockey Queen   C) Marathon Lady   D) Indian Express

Show Answer

A) The Sprint Queen (Payyoli Express)
