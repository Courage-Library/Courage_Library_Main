Traditional Indian Games

Delhi Police & SSC Exams - GK Section

Introduction

India has a rich tradition of indigenous games that reflect culture, discipline, teamwork and strength. Kabaddi, Kho-Kho, Mallakhamb, Gilli-Danda, Kushti (Wrestling) inspire modern sports and are celebrated internationally.

Memory Trick: K–K–M–G–W → Kabaddi, Kho-Kho, Mallakhamb, Gilli-Danda, Wrestling. (“Khel Ki Mitti Golden Wow!”)

Kabaddi – The Game of Strength & Strategy

OriginAncient Tamil Nadu (References in Mahabharata & Buddhist texts)
ObjectiveTouch opponents and return home without being caught
TypeTeam sport (7 players per side)
CourtRectangular – divided into two halves
Famous Leagues / EventsPro Kabaddi League (since 2014)
Popular TournamentsAsian Games, World Cup, National Kabaddi Championship
Governing BodyAmateur Kabaddi Federation of India (AKFI)
Achievements:
  • India won all Kabaddi World Cups (Men’s) from 2004 to 2016.
  • Women’s Kabaddi Team also won multiple Asian Games golds.

Kabaddi – Catch, Breathe, Conquer.

Kho-Kho – The Game of Chase

OriginMaharashtra (Derived from “Rathera” game)
ObjectiveChase and tag opponents
Players12 (9 active + 3 substitutes)
Court29m × 16m rectangular field
Major TournamentsKho-Kho Federation Cup, Ultimate Kho-Kho League (2022)
Governing BodyKho-Kho Federation of India (KKFI)
Special Facts:
  • Introduced in 1936 Berlin Olympics (Demonstration Sport)
  • Maharashtra & Telangana dominate nationals

Kho-Kho – Chase and Touch, Fast as Lightning.

Mallakhamb – Art of Strength & Balance

OriginMaharashtra, Peshwa Period (18th century)
Meaning“Malla”=Wrestler, “Khamb”=Pole
TypeGymnastics on wooden pole or rope
EquipmentVertical wooden pole (8.5 ft)
Major EventsNational Mallakhamb Championship
RecognitionState Sport of Madhya Pradesh
Variants:
  • Pole Mallakhamb
  • Rope Mallakhamb
  • Hanging Mallakhamb

Mallakhamb = India’s Ancient Gymnastics.

Gilli-Danda — The Rural Baseball

OriginRural India (2500+ years)
Similar ToCricket & Baseball
EquipmentGilli (small stick), Danda (long stick)
ObjectiveStrike gilli with danda, score by distance
PopularityCommon in India/Nepal/Bangladesh villages
Current StatusInformal, recreational, village sport

Gilli-Danda – Mini Cricket of Villages.

Wrestling (Kushti / Pehlwani) – Akhara Tradition

OriginAncient India (Mahabharata & Ramayana)
ArenaMud pit (Akhara)
TypeCombat sport, pin opponent down
Popular StylesKushti, Freestyle, Greco-Roman
Famous TournamentsBharat Kesari Dangal, Rustam-e-Hind
Famous Indian WrestlersGreat Gama, Sushil Kumar, Bajrang Punia, Vinesh Phogat
Facts:
  • The Great Gama (Ghulam Mohammad) — undefeated world champion (1910s–1940s)
  • India excels at Wrestling in Asian & Commonwealth Games

Kushti – Strength, Soil, and Spirit.

PYQs (Delhi Police, SSC & State Exams)

Which of the following is India’s indigenous game?

A) Baseball   B) Kho-Kho   C) Basketball   D) Rugby

Show Answer

B) Kho-Kho

Pro Kabaddi League was started in —

A) 2012   B) 2014   C) 2016   D) 2018

Show Answer

B) 2014

The term “Akhara” is associated with —

A) Judo   B) Boxing   C) Wrestling (Kushti)   D) Mallakhamb

Show Answer

C) Wrestling (Kushti)

Mallakhamb is performed on —

A) Rope or Pole   B) Mat or Floor   C) Net   D) Table

Show Answer

A) Rope or Pole

Kho-Kho originated from which Indian state?

A) Punjab   B) Maharashtra   C) Tamil Nadu   D) Haryana

B) Maharashtra

A) Archery   B) Wrestling   C) Fencing   D) Boxing

A) Kabaddi   B) Mallakhamb   C) Kho-Kho   D) Wrestling

B) Mallakhamb

“The Great Gama” is associated with which sport?
