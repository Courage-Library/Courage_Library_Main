Miscellaneous Awards

Delhi Police & SSC Exams – GK Section

Introduction

Besides Nobel or Booker, many miscellaneous awards often feature in GK papers – covering environment, UNESCO, beauty pageants, and recent winners.

Mnemonic: E-U-P-R → Environment, UNESCO, Pageant, Recent.
Every Unique Pageant Recognised.

International Environment Awards – Champions of the Earth (UNEP)

The Champions of the Earth is the UN’s most important global environmental award, presented by the UN Environment Programme (UNEP).

  • 2024 Winner: Dr. Madhav Gadgil (India), Lifetime Achievement
  • 2022 Winners: Dr. Purnima Devi Barman, Sir Partha Dasgupta (India)
Trick: Champions of the Earth honor those who champion the planet.

UNESCO & Other International Awards

  • UNESCO World Heritage Sites
  • UNESCO literacy and culture prizes
  • Environmental/social awards with UNESCO mandates
Trick: UNESCO – Universal Support for Education, Science & Culture.
