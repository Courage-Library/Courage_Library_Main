Puzzle & Seating Arrangement

Delhi Police Exam

1. What are Puzzles & Seating Arrangement?

Puzzles & Seating Arrangement involve arranging people, objects, or items based on given conditions in specific patterns or positions. These questions test your logical reasoning and analytical skills.

Logical Arrangement

Condition-Based Solving

Analytical Thinking

Multiple Constraints

Simple Definition: Puzzles and seating arrangements are logical problems where you arrange elements according to given conditions to find specific positions or relationships.

6. Solved Examples

Example 1: Linear Arrangement

Six people A, B, C, D, E, F sit in a row facing North. A sits third from left. B sits immediate right of A. C sits at extreme right end. D sits immediate left of F. Who sits between B and F?

Step 1: Position 1: ?, Position 2: ?, Position 3: A, Position 4: B, Position 5: ?, Position 6: C

Step 2: D sits immediate left of F, so D and F are together

Step 3: Remaining positions 1,2,5 for D, E, F

Step 4: D and F must be in positions 1-2 or 5-6, but position 6 is C

Step 5: So D and F in positions 1-2: Position 1: D, Position 2: F

Step 6: Position 5: E. Between B (position 4) and F (position 2) is position 3: A

Answer: A sits between B and F

Example 2: Circular Arrangement

Six people sit around a circular table facing center. A sits opposite B. C sits immediate right of A. D sits immediate left of B. E sits between C and D. Who sits between A and E?

Step 1: Draw circle with 6 positions. A opposite B

Step 2: C immediate right of A

Step 3: D immediate left of B

Step 4: E between C and D

Step 5: Final arrangement: A-C-E-D-B-F (clockwise)

Step 6: Between A and E is C

Answer: C sits between A and E

Example 3: Floor-based Puzzle

Six people live on 6 different floors (1-6). A lives above B. C lives immediately above D. E lives on top floor. F lives below B. Who lives on floor 3?

Step 1: Floor 6: E (given)

Step 2: C immediately above D, so C-D are together

Step 3: A above B, F below B

Step 4: Possible arrangement: Floor 6: E, Floor 5: A, Floor 4: B, Floor 3: F, Floor 2: C, Floor 1: D

Step 5: Check all conditions: A above B ✓, C above D ✓, E top ✓, F below B ✓

Answer: F lives on floor 3
